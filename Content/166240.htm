Facility - Static (Quantum)

Use the Default perspective's Facility page to access and maintain approved Facility records in the Quantum database.

NOTE: If the Replication Service is running, these records can also be viewed (but not edited) on the FIS Quantum Analytics perspective page, where:

  • only Name and Code fields show, and
  • the field values respectively correspond to extensions of the Name and Description fields in Quantum, where the extension in each case is an @ symbol followed by the Counterparty name.

A Facility is a formal business relationship governing transactions with a syndicate or single counterparty, with documented restrictions, utilization levels, and so on. Facilities generally relate to Capital Market activities and would usually therefore reflect borrowing relationships at a detailed level; they are more formal and detailed than a Dealing Line.

A Facility (or a Dealing Line) can be established for the following types of Quantum deal:

  • Cross Currency Swap
  • Currency Option
  • FRA
  • Futures
  • FX
  • Interest Rate Option
  • Interest Rate Swap
  • Letter of Credit
  • Money Market
  • Repo
  • Security

Relationship Rule records define the types of relationship (Facility, Dealing Line, both, or none) that may be associated - at deal entry time - with specific deal types, counterparties, instruments, and entities. They are not mandatory: where no matching rule applies to a new deal, any matching Facility (or Dealing Line) may be selected, or none.

You may select a Facility that matches a new deal if:

  • a matching Relationship Rule applies, and its Relationship Type is either FACILITY or ALL, or
  • no matching Relationship Rule applies.

When a valid Facility is selected and the deal is saved, the deal details are validated against the relevant settings of that Facility record*. Where differences are detected, warnings are displayed, and alerts created in the Event Log.

*NOTES:

  • The deal's effective Maturity Date (the effective date of the last deal map item) is used for validating against the Facility's applicable Limit time band to find the limit amount.
  • Validation against Counterparty Limits for any breaches is NOT performed at deal save time. Use Limits Query to monitor utilization of these limits.

To record deals against a Facility, you must create individual transaction-level Facility records. For analysis and reporting purposes, facilities can be arranged in a hierarchical structure of groups. Individual Facility records may then be added to groups as required. However, deals cannot be recorded against groups.

If Analysis Codes have been set up with a Scope setting of Facility, they will appear in the Analysis Codes tab of the Facility page. For more details, see Setting Up Analysis Codes.
