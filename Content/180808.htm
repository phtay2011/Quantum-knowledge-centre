Adding Reconciliation Adjustments

This section covers the various types of adjustments you can manually make in Prior Day and Intraday Matching (see Adjustments in Intraday Matching below).

TIP: Matching can also generate automatic adjustments. See Matching Cash Flows with Bank Flows and Important Notes about Automatic Adjustments.

NOTE: When Resource Security applies and you are not Exempt:

  • If you do not have Write access to a Bank Account, you cannot make any adjustments affecting that account. The relevant menu items and buttons are disabled.
  • Resources on the relevant adjustment forms will be filtered based on your allowed access. This applies to Entity, Instrument, Bank/Counterparty, and Bank Account lookups, where they occur.

Most types of adjustment will be added to your displayed grids depending on the Match Status, your current display filtering (as for other items), and the type of adjustment, as follows.

Adjustments in Intraday Matching

In Intraday Matching, you can manually add and delete adjustments (as in Prior Day Matching).

While Intraday Balance Statement adjustments affect only Intraday Matching data, other adjustments made in Intraday Matching do affect your main Quantum data.
