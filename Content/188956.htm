Money Market & Securities Posting, Off Balance Sheet tab

Off Balance Sheet entries for Money Market deals record the contractual exposure between Deal Date and Settlement Date.

  • Capital Discount Exposure - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this entry on deal date to record the contingent position for capital discount on coupon-bearing securities. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the Capital Discount Accrual (B/S) account.

    NOTE: In Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing must be set to Settle date, Principal accounting must be set to Face Value, and Apply contingent accounting Deal to Settlement date must be selected. If Principal accounting is set to Net Value, these defaults are not used.

  • Capital Premium Exposure - Debit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this entry on deal date to record the contingent position for capital premium on coupon-bearing securities. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the Capital Premium Accrual (B/S) account.

    NOTE: In Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing must be set to Settle date, Principal accounting must be set to Face Value, and Apply contingent accounting Deal to Settlement date must be selected. If Principal accounting is set to Net Value, these defaults are not used.

  • Face Value Borrowing Exposure - Credit Off Balance Sheet account. Quantum posts this on Deal Date to record the contingent position. It is reversed on Settlement Date.
  • Face Value Investment Exposure - Debit Off Balance Sheet account. Quantum posts this on Deal Date to record the contingent position. It is reversed on Settlement Date.
  • Interest Investment Exposure - Debit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for coupon expense on coupon-bearing securities. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the Interest Expense Accrual (B/S) account.

  • Interest Income Exposure - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for coupon income on coupon-bearing securities. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the Interest Income Accrual (B/S) account.

  • Margin Expense Exposure - Debit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for deal margin expense over the base rate on coupon-bearing securities. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the Margin Expense Accrual (B/S) account.

    NOTE: In Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing must be set to Settle date and Apply contingent accounting Deal to Settlement date must be selected.

  • Margin Income Exposure - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for deal margin expense over the base rate on coupon-bearing securities. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the Margin Income Accrual (B/S) account.

    NOTE: In Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing must be set to Settle date and Apply contingent accounting Deal to Settlement date must be selected.

  • Money Market Exposure Contra - Debit or Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this entry on Deal Date and reverses it on Settlement Date to record the other side of the contingent (exposure) entry.

  • Net Value Borrowing Exposure - Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on Deal Date to record the contingent position. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

  • Net Value Investment Exposure - Debit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on Deal Date to record the contingent position. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

  • Borrowing Exposure - Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on Deal Date to record the contingent position. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

  • WTAX Expense Exposure - Debit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for Withholding Tax expense on coupon-bearing instruments. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the WTAX Expense Accrual (B/S) account.

  • WTAX Income Exposure - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for Withholding Tax income on coupon-bearing instruments. It is reversed on settlement date and posted to the WTAX on Income Accrual (B/S) account.

  • WTAX on Discount Exposure - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for Withholding Tax on Capital Discount for coupon-bearing instruments - in particular for deals based on instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option selected. This option is available only for instruments with the following settings:

    • Formula = BOND, Timing = PERIODIC, Callable option cleared

      -or-
    • Formula = FRN-CGL, Asset Backed option cleared, Multiple Rateset option cleared)
  • WTAX on Premium Exposure - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account.

    Quantum posts this on deal date to record the contingent position for Withholding Tax on Capital Premium for coupon-bearing instruments - in particular for deals based on instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option selected. For more details of that option, see on Premium Accrual above.

    The posting is reversed on settlement date and posted to the WTAX on Premium Accrual (B/S) account.
