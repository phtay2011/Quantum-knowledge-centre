Destination Account Details, Cash Transfer

Use this area to enter the details of the account the transfer is being made to, as follows.

Bank Account/Broker Account - Select one of these two to give the flow type.

Account Type (available only for a Bank Account) - select Bank or Fund to filter the Bank and Bank Account Number fields so you can select accounts of that type only.

TIP:Fund accounts are used for Money Market Funds only. Bank applies otherwise.

Bank - select the Bank, Fund Manager, or Broker who holds the destination account.

Account Number - select the account number into which the funds will be transferred. The lookup lists accounts you hold in the selected Currency with the selected Bank, Fund Manager, or Broker.

NOTE: The Source account number must not be the same as this account number.

Account Name (read-only) - the account name for the selected destination Account Number.

Bank Value Date (read-only) - the bank's reported value date for the destination account leg of the transfer. This date:

  • will be used for calculating interest on the destination account IF that account has Interest Calculation Based on set to Bank Value Date (Interest tab, Bank Account Setup)
  • initially defaults to the Settlement Date, but may be updated (to the bank's specified date) in Bank Reconciliation when the transfer cash flow is matched to an imported "bankflow"
  • The Settlement Date will be moved to the next business day if a matching Cut-off Time rule exists and the entered settlement date exceeds the date and time set out in the rule for the location's bank account.
  • may also differ on an imported transfer (for example, via QTOM)
  • never precedes the source Deal Date.

Entity - Accept the default or select another Entity against which the cash transfer is to be recorded.

  • For a transfer to take place between Entities that are both G/L Owners, or that are associated with different G/L Owners, an Inter-company bank account default record is required.
  • When the Operational Setting Display the entity's external name is enabled, a read-only field will also display the Entity's External Name.
