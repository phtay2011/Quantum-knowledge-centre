Cash Transfer Procedures

To add a new transfer deal

  1. Click New.
  2. In the header area:
    • Optionally enter a Ticket Number for transaction reference purposes.
    • Select an Instrument to populate fields with default values.
  3. On the Deal Details tab:
    • Under Amount, select a Currency and enter a Face Value.
    • Change the Deal Date if required or accept the default (today's date), and enter the Settlement Date.
    • Optionally select a Counterparty, particularly if you want to email Deal Confirmations to a contact at the destination organization (when matching Counterparty Contact and Confirmation rules exist).
    • In the Source Account Details area, enter the details of the account the transfer is being made from.
    • In the Destination Account Details area, enter the details of the account the transfer is being made to.
    • Change the Dealer if required or accept the default (you, if you are set up as a Dealer).
    • Optionally enter any relevant Comments., select a Deal Set, and/or Counterparty Reference, if required.
  4. On the Analysis Codes tab, optionally link the transfer to predefined Analysis Codes if required.
  5. Click Save. When you recall the deal:
    • A unique sequential Deal Number has automatically been assigned by the system.
    • The G/L Entries tab shows the system-generated General Ledger entries relating to the cash transfer. The account codes are sourced from the bank or broker account records, and are non-modifiable.

For details of data items on a tab

  • Click on an item or tab when the Help panel is open.

To list transfer deals - Click the button.

TIP: Click the Configure link to change list properties - see Configure Selection List Views.

To edit a transfer deal's details

  1. Click to list available transfer deals.
  2. Select the deal you want in the list.
  3. View and optionally edit the relevant data fields on the required tabs.
  4. Click Save.

To delete a transfer deal

  1. Click to list available transfer deals.
  2. Select the deal you want in the list.
  3. In the menu, select Delete.
  4. When prompted, confirm your action.
