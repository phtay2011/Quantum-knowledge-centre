Money Market & Securities Posting, Profit and Loss tab

  • Capital Discount - Debit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the capital discount on coupon-bearing securities. Capital Discount is the amount by which the traded price is below the issue price of a coupon-based instrument. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Capital Discount Accrual (B/S) account.
  • Capital Premium - Credit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the capital premium on coupon-bearing securities. Capital Premium is the amount by which the traded price exceeds the issue price on a coupon-based instrument. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Capital Premium Accrual (B/S) account.
  • Interest Expense - Debit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the:
    • Coupon expense on coupon-bearing securities
    • Implied interest expense on simple discount instruments

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Interest Expense Accrual (B/S) account.
  • Interest Income- Credit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the:
    • Coupon income on coupon-bearing securities
    • Implied interest income on simple discount instruments

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry, and contras it to the Interest Income Accrual (B/S) account.
  • Negative Interest Expense - use to set up negative posting rule interest accounts and specify negative interest income and expense accounts separately from positive interest income and expense accounts.
  • Negative Interest Income - use to set up negative posting rule interest accounts and specify negative interest income and expense accounts separately from positive interest income and expense accounts.
  • Margin Expense - Debit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the Deal margin expense over the base rate on coupon-bearing securities. If the Accrue Interest check box on the Accounting Rules tab is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry, and contras it to the Margin Expense Accrual account.
  • Margin Income - Credit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the deal margin income over the base rate on coupon-bearing securities. If the Accrue Interest check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry, and contras it to the Margin Income Accrual account.
  • Adhoc Amount - Debit/Credit Profit and Loss account. If the amount is an:
    • Adhoc Interest amount, and the Accrue adhoc amounts check box on the Accounting Rules tab is selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry.
    • Adhoc amount, or the Accrue adhoc amounts check box on the Accounting Rules tab is cleared, Quantum creates the entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
  • Issue Free Expense - Debit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the issue fee expense paid on a deal. An Issue Fee amount can be applied at deal entry in Money Market deals. If on the Accounting Rules tab, the Accrual method is Amortized Cost and Accrue issue fee is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by an Issue Fee Expense Accrual account entry, dated the deal maturity date. This is a separate entry from the settlement entry because the issue fee amount is not part of the settlement cashflow, but is a separate cashflow amount to be paid or received from the Counterparty on the deal.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry and contras it to the Issue Fee Expense Accrual (B/S) account.
  • Issue Free Income - Credit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the issue fee income received on a deal. An Issue Fee amount can be applied at deal entry in Money Market deals. If in Accounting Rules , the Accrual method is Amortized Cost and Accrue issue fee is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by an Issue Fee Income Accrual account entry, dated the deal maturity date. This is a separate entry from the settlement entry because the issue fee amount is not part of the settlement cash flow, but is a separate cash flow amount to be paid or received from the Counterparty on the deal.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry and contras it to the Issue Fee Income Accrual (B/S) account.
  • Face Value Index Adjustment - Debit/Credit Profit and Loss account. This is used for indexed bonds, currently Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities (TIPS). The Face Value Index Adjustment is the amount by which the principal of an indexed bond (excluding Capital Index Bond - Reserve Bank of Australia (CIB-RBA)) is adjusted, due to changes in the Consumer Price Index (CPI). If the Accrue face value index check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a Face Value or Net Value Investment (Balance Sheet) account entry, dated the deal maturity date.
    • Selected, Accrue Inquiry creates this entry, and contras it to the Face Value Investment (B/S) or Net Value Investment (B/S) account
  • Gain: Realized - Credit Profit and Loss account. If an instrument is closed out, then irrespective of the Posting method option selected in Accounting Rules, this default records the gain on closeout, and is posted at the time of the closeout, but dated as at the Settlement Date of the closeout. If Posting method field is set to Realized, the Revaluations posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Revaluation Gain: Realized (B/S) account. On settlement, there is no unrealized amount to reverse.

    NOTE: In Accounting Rules, either Money Market Reval Method (for Money Market deals) or Security Reval Method (for Securities deals) is set to Bills, Market, or Projected (so that Quantum then posts revaluations). Otherwise, Quantum does not post transaction revaluations, and these defaults are not used.

  • Gain: Unrealized - Credit Profit and Loss account. If the Posting method field in Accounting Rules is set to Unrealized, the Revaluations posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Revaluation Gain: Unrealized (B/S) account. On settlement, Quantum reverses the unrealized amount, and records the realized amount using the Gain: Realized (P/L) account or Loss: Realized (P/L) account, as applicable.

    NOTE: In Accounting Rules, either Money Market Reval Method (for Money Market deals) or Security Reval Method (for Securities deals) is set to Bills, Market, or Projected (so that Quantum then posts revaluations). Otherwise, Quantum does not post transaction revaluations, and these defaults are not used.

  • Loss: Realized - Debit Profit and Loss account. See Gain: Realized above.
  • Loss: Unrealized - Debit Profit and Loss account. See Gain: Unrealized above.
  • CCY Trans Gain Bank: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the differences between base currency and natural currency amounts for non-base currency bank accounts arising as a result of changes in exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Gain Bank: Unreal -
  • CCY Trans Gain Interest: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the differences between base currency and natural currency interest amounts for non-base currency deals, arising as a result of changes in exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Gain Interest: Unreal - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the unrealized differences between base currency and natural currency interest amounts for non-base currency deals, arising as a result of changes in exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Gain Principal: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the realized gains in the base currency principal amounts of non-base currency deals, arising as a result of the change in the exchange rates. This represents the difference between the principal and repay principal amounts. If the principal is accrued, this represents the difference between the accrual and settlement amounts.
  • CCY Trans Gain Principal: Unreal - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records unrealized differences in the base currency principal amounts for non-base currency deals, arising as a result of the change in the exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Loss Bank: Real - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Bank: Real above.
  • CCY Trans Loss Bank: Unreal - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Bank: Unreal above.
  • CCY Trans Loss Interest: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. Records the realized gain on base currency Unsettled Payables and Unsettled receivables accounts for non base currency deals.
  • CCY Trans Loss Interest: Unreal - Credit Profit and Loss account. Records the unrealized gain on base currency Unsettled Payables and Unsettled receivables accounts for non base currency deals.
  • CCY Trans Loss Principal: Real - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Principal: Real above.
  • CCY Trans Loss Principal: Unreal - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Principal: Unreal above.
  • CCY Trans Loss Unsettled: Real - Debit Profit and Loss account. Records the realized loss on base currency Unsettled Payables and Unsettled receivables accounts for non-base currency deals.
  • CCY Trans Loss Unsettled: Unreal - Debit Profit and Loss account. Records the unrealized loss on base currency Unsettled Payables and Unsettled receivables accounts for non-base currency deals.
  • FID Paid on Investments - FID abolished in Australia from 1 July 2001.
  • WTAX Expense - Debit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the:
    • Withholding Tax on Coupon expense on coupon-bearing instruments
    • Withholding Tax on implied Interest expense on simple discount instruments

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the WTAX Expense Accrual (B/S) account.
  • WTAX on Income - Credit Profit and Loss account. This is used for the:
    • Withholding Tax on Coupon income on coupon-bearing instruments
    • Withholding Tax on Implied interest income on simple discount instruments

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry, and contras it to the WTAX on Income Accrual (B/S) account.
  • WTAX on Premium - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used for the Withholding Tax on Capital Premium for deals based on instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option selected. This option is available only for instruments with the following settings:
    • Formula = BOND, Timing = PERIODIC, Callable option cleared

      -or-
    • Formula = FRN-CGL, Asset Backed option cleared, Multiple Rateset option cleared)

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to the WTAX on Premium Accrual (B/S) account.
  • WTAX on Discount - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used for the Withholding Tax on Capital Discount for deals based on instruments the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option selected. For more details of that option, see on Premium Accrual above. If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to WTAX on Discount Accrual (B/S) account.
  • WTAX Gain: Realized - Realized WHT Gain when closing out position.
  • WTAX Loss: Realized - Realized WHT Loss when closing out position.
