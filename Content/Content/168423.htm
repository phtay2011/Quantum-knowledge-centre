Bank Interest Statements

Use the Bank Account Interest--Query page to preview Bank Interest deal cash flows and interest postings on bank accounts or Money Market Fund accounts.

This query can be run in Reporting mode or Processing mode, depending on the menu you use when selecting the query: Reporting or Processing, respectively.

  • Use Reporting mode first, to preview Bank Interest deal cash flows* and interest postings on bank accounts or Money Market Fund accounts based on the selection criteria, without committing them (making no database changes).
  • Use Processing mode to create one or more* Bank Interest deal cash flows and to post interest on bank accounts or Money Market Fund accounts based on the selection criteria.

*TIPS: More than one Bank Interest cash flow will be created when:

  • The bank account balance changes sign during the interest period and a Gross interest calculation method is specified for the account, and/or...
  • Withholding Tax (WHT) is due on the interest.

Bank Account Interest (AI) deals created when you run this query in Processing mode can be viewed on the Cash Flows tab in Bank Interest Adjustments.

  • Whether or not interest is posted, and the posting details, are defaulted on the Bank Account; and in in order to post interest, bank interest rates must have been set up for the account in Bank Account Defaults.
  • If Bank Account Interest Accruals have been posted, the deal number reserved for the accrual amounts is used and a live AI deal created. The accrual balances are cleaned up and interest posted to P/L.

For more information

For details of options and settings relating to Bank Account interest, see Interest tab, Bank Account/Broker Account and 3rd Party Interest tab, Bank Account (for example, you can preserve interest deals and include zero balances).

For details of how Delivery Instructions are applied to Account Interest Deal cash flows, see Calculating and Posting Bank Account Interest.
