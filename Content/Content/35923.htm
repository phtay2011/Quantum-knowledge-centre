Contingent Accounting Settings (Repos Accounting Rule)

Apply from settlement date to maturity date

(Optional.)

Select this option to apply contingent accounting to Repurchase Agreement deals (Repos). The system records the intangible (forward) exposures for fee-based and stock-switch Repos where the Settlement Date is later than the Deal Date.

Contingent entries record contractual exposure between Settlement Date and Maturity Date. They are posted to the off balance sheet G/L Accounts.

When this option is selected, the following balance sheet entries are recorded on Settlement Date, for each Repo format. These are reversed on maturity of the Repurchase Agreement.

Classic

Records the contractual exposure as the settlement amount of the cash borrowed. The off balance sheet accounts used are:

Dr Switch Exposure Contra
Cr Switch Out Exposure

Reverse

Records the contractual exposure as the settlement amount of the cash lent. The off balance sheet accounts used are:

Dr Switch In Exposure
Cr Switch Exposure Contra

Fee based borrowing

Records the contractual exposure as the Face Value of the security borrowed. The off balance sheet accounts used are:

Dr Switch Exposure Contra
Cr Switch In Exposure

Fee based lending

Records the contractual exposure as the Face Value of the security being lent. The off balance sheet accounts used are:

Dr Switch Out Exposure
Cr Switch Exposure Contra

Security borrowing

Records the contractual exposure of both the security borrowed and the collateral at Face Value.

The off balance sheet accounts used are:

  • Security Borrowed:

    Dr Switch Exposure Contra
    Cr Switch In Exposure

  • Collateral:

    Dr Switch Out Exposure
    Cr Switch Exposure Contra

Security lending

Records the contractual exposure of both the security lent and the collateral at Face Value.

The off balance sheet accounts used are:

  • Security Lent:

    Dr Switch Out Exposure
    Cr Switch Position

  • Collateral:

    Dr Switch Exposure Contra
    Cr Switch In Exposure

Buy/Sell Back and Sell/Buy Back

Contingent Accounting entries can be posted to the Off Balance Sheet for these Repo formats.

NOTE: These entries are posted only where the underlying security uses the Bond Periodic formula.

The available entries are for:

  • Face Value Investment Exposure
  • Face Value Borrowing Exposure
  • Net Value Investment Exposure
  • Net Value Borrowing Exposure
  • Interest Expense Exposure
  • Interest Income Exposure
  • Capital Discount Exposure
  • Capital Premium Exposure
  • RA Receipt Exposure Contra
  • RA Payment Exposure Contra

The entries created will depend on the accounting and accrual methods for the underlying security of these Repo formats. These are sourced from settings on the Money Market & Securities tab for the applicable Accounting Rule - in particular, Principal Accounting and capital and interest Accrual Settings.
