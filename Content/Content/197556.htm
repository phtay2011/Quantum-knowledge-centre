Compound Factor Rounding - Yield CDI formulas

You can now specify Compound Factor Rounding for the following deal types when based on instruments with YIELD CDI Formula Timing selections, where default settings can be entered on those instruments, as listed below.

  • For Cross Currency Swap and Interest Rate Swap:
    • instruments* - you can enter a default Compound Factor Rounding Method on the Pay Pricing tab and/or Receive Pricing tab when Formula Timing is YIELD - CDI ON MATURITY for the corresponding leg (Pay Details or Receive Details tab); and for
    • deals - the mandatory Compound Factor Rounding Method appears on the Instrument Details tab's Pay and/or Receive area when Formula Timing is YIELD - CDI ON MATURITY for the corresponding leg (Deal Details tab), defaulting to the instrument setting in each case.
  • For Money Market and Securities:
    • instruments* - you can enter a default Compound Factor Rounding Method on the Details tab when Formula Timing is YIELD - CDI PERIODIC or YIELD - CDI ROLLING; and for
    • deals - the mandatory Compound Factor Rounding Method appears on the Other Details tab when the underlying Instrument's Formula Timing is YIELD - CDI PERIODIC or YIELD - CDI ROLLING, defaulting to the instrument setting (if any).

The usual rounding options are available in each case - namely: NO ROUNDING, ROUND (5/4), ROUND UP, or ROUND DOWN.. And when ROUND (5/4), ROUND UP, or ROUND DOWN is selected, enter a value from 0 to 16 in the adjacent field to set the number of decimal places you want to round to.

*NOTE: If you have upgraded from an earlier release, the settings for pre-existing instrument records will be ROUND (5/4) and 16 places.

Previously, Quantum rounded the Compound Factor to 16 decimal places and you could not change this.
