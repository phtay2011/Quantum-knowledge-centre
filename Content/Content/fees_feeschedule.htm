Fee Deal page

Use this page to create and maintain parameters for applying fees to Quantum Money Market, Securities, and/or Interest Rate Swap deals only.

NOTES: This page (unlike most other Dealing pages):

  • uses its own set of system-assigned Deal Numbers, distinct from those across all "standard" deals; and
  • has no Deal Map, Cash Flows, nor G/L Entries tabs (because none of those apply). Fee deal records provide settings and schedules for generating Actual Cash Flow deals containing fee cash flows and G/L entries via the Process Fees page.

To list fee deal records - Click the button. (Click the Configure link to change browse list properties.)

To add a new fee deal record - Click New; optionally enter a Ticket Number and External Name; select Fee Name, Currency, Revenue/Expense, and Entity; enter Deal Date, Start Date, and Maturity Date. Then:

  • in the Calculation tab menu, select New and assign Base Templates and/or Formulas to one or more date ranges (restrictions apply);
  • on the Other Details tab, specify settings for the Actual Cash Flow deals that will be generated for fee amounts, the locations on which to base non-business day calendars for fees-related dates, and (optionally) the estimation types and related parameters for True-Ups;
  • on the Schedule tab, define the frequency of the scheduled dates for fee calculation, payment, invoicing, and base amount resets;
  • on the Accounting tab, specify the Chart of Account codes required for G/L entries on accrual, invoice, calculation, and payment dates; and
  • click Save.

To change a record's details - Select it in the browse list, edit the relevant items in the header and on the required tabs as above, and click Save.

To delete a record in full - Select it in the browse list and select Delete in the page menu. When prompted, confirm.

To go to a related Fee page - In the page menu under RELATED, select Base Template, Fee Formula, or Process Fees, as required.
