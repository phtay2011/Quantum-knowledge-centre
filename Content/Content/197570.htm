Change Our Account function (Settlement Processing)

Use this grid menu function to change "Our" Bank Account for multiple selected cash flows over one or more accounts, to a single account. You require Our Instructions Module Security rights for the Settlements module (or are exempt), and your selected items (including any netted flows) are subject to the following restrictions:

  • Type is CASH or NET,
  • all have the same Currency,
  • none are linked to a stock flow;
  • Flow Status is UNACTIONED,
  • none are exception flows (Pay/Rec is NOT Exception nor Canceled),
  • Transaction Type is NOT Bank Account Transfer, and
    • cannot be Futures Deals if you want to change to a Bank or Money Market Fund account, but
    • can only be Futures Deals if you want to change to a Broker account.

Resource security applies as usual.

Procedure summary

  1. Select the items you want to change.

    TIP: Applying View Filters and changing the sort column can help when you have many items to select.

  2. In the grid menu, select Change Our Account. (The function will only be available if your selections meet the requirements above.)

    The Change Our Account dialog box is displayed.

  3. Select the new Bank, Broker, or Money Market Fund Account Number.

    Only in-use accounts in the currency of your selected items are available for selection, and Broker accounts are not available unless only Futures items are selected (when only Broker accounts are available.

  4. Click OK.

    The new account is applied to the selected items.

    The Operational Setting Apply new payment method when changing our instructions is observed as follows:

    • if enabled - the Payment Method specified on the new bank account as the default is observed, or
    • if disabled - the Payment Method remains unchanged on each cash flow.
