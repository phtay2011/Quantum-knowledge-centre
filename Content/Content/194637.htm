Bond Basis support for Yield Periodic and P&I deals

(First introduced in Release SP 6.7.1, August 2018.)

You can now apply a bond basis coupon calculation to YIELD PERIODIC and PRINCIPAL & INTEREST Security and Money Market deals, so that even interest payments can be calculated.

Implementation

On the Security and Money Market Instrument pages, the Coupon Type field now appears (on the Pricing tab) when:

  • Formula Timing is set to YIELD PERIODIC, PRINCIPAL & INTEREST ACTUAL END DATE, or PRINCIPAL & INTEREST NOTIONAL END DATE (Details tab), and
  • Day Count is ACTUAL/ACTUAL (Dates tab).

In the Coupon Type field, select the default coupon type for deals based on this instrument - one of the following:

  • EQUAL - to take the annual coupon amount and divide it equally by the number of coupon periods within the annual period, irrespective of the number of days within the coupon period; or...
  • EXACT - to base coupons on the actual number of days in the coupon period (the default and pre-existing method).
