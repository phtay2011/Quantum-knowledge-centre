Accrued interest calculation, ACTUAL/360

For accrued interest calculations, Equal and Exact coupon calculation results are the same.

Regular coupon

In this example:

  • Previous coupon date is 15 Feb
  • Settlement Date is 23 Mar

Equal

Exact

(As for Exact coupon)

Accrued interest 15 Feb - 23 Mar

10%*(36/360) = 1.00%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 36 = Number of days 15 Feb - 23 Mar

Short First coupon

In this example:

  • Dated Date is 17 Nov
  • First Coupon Date is 15 Feb
  • Settlement Date is 23 Mar

Equal

Exact

(As for Exact coupon)

Accrued interest 17 Nov - 15 Feb

10%*(67/360)=1.861111%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 67 = Number of days 17 Nov - 15 Feb

Long First coupon

In this example:

  • Dated Date is 17 Nov
  • First Coupon Date is 15 Aug
  • Settlement Date is 23 Mar

Equal

Exact

(As for Exact coupon)

Odd period accrued 17 Nov - 15 Feb

10%*(90/360)=2.50%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 90 = Number of days 17 Nov - 15 Feb

(As for Exact coupon)

Regular period accrued 15 Feb - 23 March

10%*(36/365)=0.986301%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 36 = Number of days 15 Feb - 23 Mar

(As for Exact coupon)

Total accrued (Odd plus regular period)

2.5+1.0 = 3.50%

Short Last coupon

In this example:

  • Penultimate Coupon Date is 15 Aug
  • Final Coupon Date is 17 Nov (Regular cycle is 15 Aug - 15 Feb)
  • Settlement Date is 23 Oct

Equal

Exact

(As for Exact coupon)

Short period accrued 15 Aug - 23 Oct

10%*(69/360)=1.916667%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 69= Number of days 15 Aug - 23 Oct

Long Last coupon

In this example:

  • Penultimate Coupon Date is 15 Feb
  • Final Coupon Date is 17 Nov (regular cycle 15 Aug / 15 Feb)
  • Settlement Date is 23 Oct.

Equal

Exact

(As for Exact coupon)

Regular period accrued 15 Feb - 15Aug

10%*(181/360) = 5.027778%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 181 = Number of days 15 Feb - 15 Aug

(As for Exact coupon)

Odd period accrued 15 Aug - 23 Oct

10%*(69/360) = 1.916667%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 69 = Number of days 15 Aug - 23 Oct

(As for Exact coupon)

Total accrued (Odd plus regular period)

5.027778+1.916667% = 6.944445%
