Our Instructions function (Cash Flow)

(Quick menu function on a single Cash Flow item or a Netted group.)

For a cash flow (or Net), Our Instructions are those concerning the bank account you are paying from or receiving cash into.

You can view and possibly change these instructions from the Settlements page results grid by clicking a linked value in the appropriate Cash flow grid row and selecting Our Instructions from the Quick menu. A separate Our Instructions window opens for the purpose.

TIP: To change Our Account for multiple items (subject to restrictions), use the Change Our Account function.

You can also access Our Instructions from the Settlement Approvals page in the same way, but for viewing only.

For a cash flow:

  • You can optionally change the Account, Payment Method, Entity Identifier, Delivery Narrative text, and Delivery Details option.
  • If the deal is CLS-eligible, and CLS is mandatory, you cannot change to an account that would make the deal non-eligible.
  • You cannot change Our Delivery Instructions for linked flows (cash and stock) that have a Payment Sent status of YES (Deliver/Receive against Payment).

When selecting a cash flow in the Settlement results grid:

  • if you select a cash flow with a currency that is part of the Euro Currency Group, the currency for that cash flow will be converted to the Euro, or to another Euro Currency Group member, and the Account drop-down list will include all accounts that have Euro Group currencies.

Field Descriptions

You can change the following items (when this form is accessed from Settlements - not from Settlement Approvals).

Account

If you change the account:

  • The Payment Method for that flow may or may not change, as follows*:
    • If the system-wide option Apply New Payment Method when Changing Our Instructions is selected in Operational Settings (under Bank Accounts), the Payment Method does not change in all cases - even if it is not an Allowable Method on the new bank account (Payment Details tab).
    • If the Apply New Payment Method when Changing Our Instructions option is cleared in Operational Settings:
      • If the Payment Method for the original cash flow is an Allowable Method on the new bank account, the Payment Method is NOT changed.
      • If the Payment Method for the original cash flow is not an Allowable Method on the new bank account (Payment Details tab), the Payment Method is either changed to the Default Payment Method from the new account, or it is left blank if there are no Allowable Methods on the new account.
      • If there is no Payment Method on the original cash flow, the Payment Method defaults to the Default Payment Method (if one exists) on the new account.

      *NOTE: You can still change or clear the Payment Method before saving the changed instructions, if required.

  • If you change the Account in a netted group on:
    • the group's header row - your change will apply to all cash flows in the netted group, and the group is preserved, then...
    • an individual cash flow in a netted group - only that flow is changed, and it is removed from the netted group
  • If the deal is CLS-eligible, and CLS is:
    • mandatory - you cannot change to an account that would make the deal non-eligible, but if
    • optional - you may change to an account that would make the deal non-eligible (a warning message will display and you can continue or cancel).

Delivery Narrative

(Applies to cash flows only)

Enter your free-format narrative text* in the text box and select how your entered text will be output - one of.

  • Add To Account Details - add your entered text to any existing default narrative for the account, or
  • Replace Account Details - output your entered text instead of any existing default narrative for the account.

* You should restrict the text to codes that are acceptable to the applicable banking system. You may enter multiple lines, and any continuation lines must start with two slashes (//). The output is restricted to four lines of up to 35 characters each, with any longer lines truncated, and any invalid codes ignored.

Payment Method

(May be changed for cash flows or non-Maturity stock flows.)

If required, select a different payment method from those valid for the bank or stock account, as applicable.

Entity Identifier

Identifiers are used to identify the entity at the bank or clearing house when using external confirmations and EFT systems.

If required, select a different identifier from those available for the selected Payment Method and the transaction's G/L Owner.

See About Entities and Business Structure for more information about creating and changing entity identifiers.

Third Party Instructions

(Our Instructions window menu)

These values are not included in any confirmation or EFT output.

Generally, Third Party Instructions will not apply to Our Instructions, but to 'Theirs' - see Counterparty Instructions.
