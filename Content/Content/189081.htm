Adding an Ad Hoc Rateset (FRNs only)

You can define ad hoc Ratesets between Coupon Dates for the FRN-CGL formula on Money Market or Security deals, without flagging such deals as Customized - Major.

Prerequisites

In the underlying Instrument:

  • Formula must be set to FRN-CGL,
  • Allow asset backed must not be selected, and
  • Allow Multiple Rateset must be selected (on the Dates tab). This means that Ratesets can occur more frequently than Coupons. This is necessary to support ad hoc Ratesets without needing Level 2 Customization.

Procedure Summary

To add an Ad Hoc Rateset to the current deal

  1. On the Deal Map tab, select New from the grid menu.

    The New Map Items dialog box opens. For more details, see Customizing Money Market Deals.

  2. Enter the required Effective Date and Action Date.
  3. In the Component lookup, select RATE SET, and enter the rate in the Amount field.
  4. Click OK.

    The dialog box closes.

  5. Click Save.
  6. In the page menu,select Rate Set.

    The Rate Set dialog box opens. For more details, see Rate Sets.

  7. Click Save.

    The Rate Set dialog box closes.

    The correct interest cash flows based upon the Effective dates, rates, and principal flows are now displayed.

  8. On the Deal Details tab's Deal Interest / Pricing Information panel, edit the Income field if required, to specify the correct income number.

    (Required when there is more than one Rateset prior to the Settlement Date.)

  9. Save the deal.

    The correct settlement amount is now shown on the Cashflows tab.

Important Notes

After ad hoc ratesets have been entered:

  • Amortized Cost continues to be supported, and
  • Closeouts and Unwinds are also supported.

The pricing formula for FRN-CGL continues to assume that the Rateset frequency equals the Coupon frequency.
