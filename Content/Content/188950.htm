Money Market & Securities Posting, Balance Sheet tab

  • Issue Fee Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account. When, in Accounting Rules, the Accrual method is Amortized Cost and Accrue issue fee is selected, then on deal G/L creation Issue Fee Expense Accrual (B/S) is posted and is expensed to the Profit and Loss account each time accruals are processed. Leave Accrue issue feecleared to expense the amount at maturity.

    NOTE:

    • The issue fee amount is not part of the settlement cash flow, but is a separate cash flow amount to be paid or received from the Counterparty on the deal.
    • If the Accrual method field is set to Straight Line, Yield Cum, or Yield Ex, the Accrue Issue Fee field is not available.
  • Issue Fee Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. When, on the Accounting Rules tab, the the Accrual method is Amortized Cost and Accrue issue fee is selected, then on deal G/L creation Issue Fee Income Accrual (B/S) is posted and is expensed to the Profit and Loss account each time accruals are processed. Leave Accrue issue fee cleared to expense the amount.

    NOTE:

    • The issue fee amount is not part of the settlement cash flow, but is a separate cash flow amount to be paid or received from the Counterparty on the deal.
    • If the Accrual method field is set to Straight Line, Yield Cum, or Yield Ex, the Accrue Issue Fee field is not available.
  • Face Value Borrowing - Credit Balance Sheet Liability account. If Interest is prepaid (for example, discounted), Quantum posts the Face Value of the instrument to this account, and debits the Prepaid Discount to the Interest Expense Accrual (B/S) account.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Principal accounting must be set to Face Value (if set to Net Value, these defaults are not used).

  • Face Value Investments - Debit Balance Sheet Asset account. If Interest is prepaid (for example, discounted), Quantum posts the Face Value of the instrument to this account, and credits the Unearned Income to the Interest Expense Accrual (B/S) account.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Principal accounting must be set to Face Value (if set to Net Value, these defaults are not used).

  • Net Value Borrowing - Credit Balance Sheet Liability account. If Interest is prepaid (for example, discounted), Quantum posts the Face Value less the interest deducted to this account. In addition, if the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum debits the interest to the Interest Expense (P/L) account at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry, dated the deal maturity date. For Bond-type instruments, the amount posted includes any Capital Discount/Premium.
    • Selected, the Accrual posting report posts accrual entries to this account, and to the Interest/Coupon Expense (P/L) account.

      NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Principal accounting must be set to Net Value (if set to Face Value, these defaults are not used).

  • Net Value Investment - Debit Balance Sheet Asset account. If Interest is prepaid (for example, discounted), Quantum posts the Face Value less the Interest deducted to this account. In addition, if the Accrue interest check box Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum debits the Interest to the Interest Expense (P/L) account at deal save time and contras it by a bank account entry, dated the deal maturity date. For Bond-type instruments, the amount posted includes any Capital Discount/Premium.
    • Selected, the Accrual posting report posts accrual entries to this account, and to the Interest/Coupon Expense (P/L) account.

      NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Principal accounting must be set to Net Value (if set to Face Value, these defaults are not used).

  • Capital Discount Deferred - Credit Balance Sheet account. When, in Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing is set to Deal date, Capital Discount deferred in the Revaluation process will be recorded.
  • Capital Premium Deferred - Debit Balance Sheet account. When, in Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing is set to Deal date, Capital Premium deferred in the Revaluation process will be recorded.
  • Unsettled Payables - Credit Balance Sheet liability account. When, in Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing is set to Deal Date, the amount of the bank account entry on Deal Date is recorded. When settlement occurs, this amount is reversed and posted to the required bank account.
  • Unsettled Receivables - Debit Balance Sheet asset account. When, in Accounting Rules, Balance sheet timing is set to Deal Date, the amount of the bank account entry on the Deal Date is recorded. When settlement occurs, this amount is reversed and posted to the required bank account.
  • Money Market Position - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used for Dual Currency Bonds as the contra entry for the Bond Income on settlement, when it is converted into the coupon currency.
  • Adhoc Amount Accrual - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. You can customize deals to insert additional cash flows. In addition to Principal and Interest, you can classify these amounts as Adhoc Amounts or Adhoc Interest. These amounts are intended to be Profit and Loss items rather than capital. If the Accrue adhoc amounts check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, this default is not used and the ad hoc amount is posted directly to Profit and Loss (Adhoc Amount default).
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. The accruals posting report will post from this account to the Adhoc Amount default (Profit and Loss tab).

      NOTE:Adhoc Interest amounts follow the Accounting Rule for Accrue Interest and post to the Interest Income Accrual or Interest Expense Accrual default account or straight to the Interest Expense or Interest Income account in the Profit and Loss.

  • Capital Discount Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue capital discount on coupon-bearing securities. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • Capital Premium Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue capital premium on coupon-bearing securities. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • Interest Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue the:
    • Coupon expense on coupon-bearing securities
    • Implied interest expense on simple discount instruments
    • Ad hoc interest items

    Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • Negative Interest Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue the:
    • Coupon expense on coupon-bearing securities - for negative interest
    • Implied interest expense on simple discount instruments - for negative interest
    • Ad hoc interest items - for negative interest

    Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.

  • Interest Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue:
    • Coupon income on coupon-bearing securities
    • Implied interest income on simple discount instruments
    • Ad hoc interest items

    Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • Negative Interest Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue:
    • Coupon income on coupon-bearing securities - for negative interest rates
    • Implied interest income on simple discount instruments - for negative interest rates
    • Ad hoc interest items - for negative interest rates

    Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.

  • Margin Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue the deal margin over the base rate on coupon-bearing securities. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. It is expensed to the Profit and Loss account each time accruals are processed.
  • Margin Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is used to accrue the deal margin over the base rate on coupon-bearing securities. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. It is expensed to the Profit and Loss account each time accruals are processed.
  • Revaluation Gain: Realized - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where Quantum posts a Gain: Realized (P/L) entry.
  • Revaluation Gain: Unrealized - Debit Balance Sheet account.
  • Revaluation Loss: Realized - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where Quantum posts a Loss: Realized (P/L) entry.
  • Revaluation Loss: Unrealized - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where Quantum posts a Loss: Unrealized (P/L) entry.
  • FID Payable on Borrowing - - FID abolished in Australia from 1 July 2001.
  • Tax Credit - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used to represent the Withholding Tax deducted on interest received - that is, any Tax Credit.
  • WTAX Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. This is used to accrue the:
    • Withholding Tax on Coupon Expense on coupon-bearing instruments
    • Withholding Tax on implied Interest Expense on simple discount instruments

      NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • WTAX on Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. This is used to accrue:
    • Withholding Tax on Coupon income on coupon-bearing instruments
    • Withholding Tax on Implied interest income on simple discount instruments

      NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • WTAX Payable - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is used to represent the Withholding Tax payable to the Tax authority on interest paid.

    NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • WTAX on Premium Accrual - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. This is used to accrue Withholding Tax on Capital Premium for deals based on instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option selected. This option is available only for instruments with the following settings:
    • Formula = BOND, Timing = PERIODIC, Callable option cleared

      -or-
    • Formula = FRN-CGL, Asset Backed option cleared, Multiple Rateset option cleared)

      NOTE: To use these values, in Accounting Rules, Accrue capital must be selected (if cleared, these defaults are not used).

  • WTAX on Discount Accrual - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. This is used to accrue Withholding Tax on Capital Discount for deals based on instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option selected. For more details of that option, see on Premium Accrual above.
  • VAT Credit - Debit Balance Sheet account. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. This is used to post VAT (Value Added Tax) payments for deals based on instruments with a VAT tax type selected.

    NOTE:VAT cash flows will not be accrued.

  • VAT Payable - Credit VAT receipts to a Liability Balance Sheet account. Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry. This is used to post VAT (Value Added Tax) receipts for deals based on instruments with a VAT tax type selected.

    NOTE:VAT cash flows will not be accrued.
