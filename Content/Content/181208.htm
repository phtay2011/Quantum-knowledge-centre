Postings when Accruing Interest (net of WHT)

This topic covers postings made for deals supporting Philippines financial market conventions requiring Withholding Tax (WHT) to be applied to Philippines Government-issued PHP-denominated Fixed Rate Bonds and FRNs. They may also apply to other jurisdictions.

When Accrue interest income/expense is selected and the underlying instrument has the Calculate WHT on accrued income or Calculate WHT on capital discount or premium option selected, special postings will be made for any WHT on Accrued Interest (Purchased Income) and/or Discount/Premium. These options apply only to instruments with the following settings:

  • Formula = FRN-CGL PERIODIC, Allow asset backed option cleared, Allow multiple rateset option cleared), or
  • FormulaTiming = BOND PERIODIC, Callable option cleared (Securities only)

NOTE: NO WHT postings will be made if no WHT Rate is entered on the deal (Other Details tab).

Treatment for Accrued Interest (Purchased Income)

The Calculate WHT on accrued income option is selected on the underlying instrument.

For an Investment (at par):

  • On Settlement Date:

    CR B/S Bank Account
    DR B/S FV Investment
    DR B/S Interest Income Accrual
    DR B/S WTax on Income Accrual*
    CR B/S Tax Credit*

    Settlement amount
    Net amount
    Purchased Income Net of WHT
    WHT on Purchased Income
    Income settled Net of WHT

  • 1-day Accrual:

    DR B/S Interest Income Accrual
    CR P&L Interest Income
    DR B/S WTax on Income Accrual
    CR P&L WTax on Income

    Interest Accrual
    "
    WHT Accrual
    "

  • On First Coupon Date:

    DR B/S Bank Account
    CR B/S Interest Income Accrual
    DR B/S Tax Credit
    CR B/S WTax on Income Accrual

    Coupon Amount
    "
    Coupon WHT
    "

    NOTE: If WTax on Income Accrual and Tax Credit point to the same account on the Deal page's G/L Entries tab, these entries will net to zero within the ledger.

For an Borrowing (at par):

  • On Settlement Date:

    DR B/S Bank Account
    CR B/S FV Borrowing
    CR B/S Interest Expense Accrual
    CR B/S WTax on Expense Accrual
    DR B/S Tax Credit

    Settlement amount
    Net amount
    Purchased Income Net of WHT
    WHT on Purchased Income
    Income settled Net of WHT

    NOTE: If WTax on Expense Accrual and Tax Credit point to the same account on the Deal page's G/L Entries tab, these entries will net to zero within the ledger.

  • 1-day Accrual:

    DR P&L Interest Expense
    CR B/S Interest Expense Accrual
    DR P&L WTax on Expense
    CR B/S WTax on Expense Accrual

    Interest Accrual
    "
    WHT Accrual
    "

  • On First Coupon Date:

    CR B/S Bank Account
    DR B/S Interest Expense Accrual
    CR B/S Bank Account
    DR B/S WTax on Expense Accrual

    Coupon Amount
    "
    Coupon WHT
    "

Treatment for Capital Discount

The Calculate WHT on capital discount or premium option is selected on the underlying instrument.

For an Investment dealt at a Discount:

  • On Settlement Date:

    DR B/S FV Investment
    CR B/S Bank Account
    CR B/S Capital Discount Accrual
    DR B/S WTax on Discount Accrual

    Settlement amount
    Net amount
    Discount
    WHT on Discount

    The WTax on Discount Accrual adjusts the overall Settlement value.

For an Borrowing dealt at a Discount:

  • On Settlement Date:

    CR B/S FV Borrowing
    DR B/S Bank Account
    DR B/S Capital Discount Accrual
    CR B/S WTax on Discount Accrual

    Settlement amount
    Net amount
    Discount
    WHT on Discount

Treatment for Capital Premium

The Calculate WHT on capital discount or premium option is selected on the underlying instrument.

For an Investment dealt at a Premium:

  • On Settlement Date:

    CR B/S Bank Account
    DR B/S FV Investment
    DR B/S Capital Premium Accrual
    CR B/S WTax on Premium Accrual

    Settlement amount
    Net amount
    Premium
    WHT on Premium

    The WTax on Premium Accrual adjusts the overall total to match the Settlement value.

For an Borrowing dealt at a Premium:

  • On Settlement Date:

    DR B/S Bank Account
    CR B/S FV Borrowing
    CR B/S Capital Premium Accrual
    DR B/S WTax on Premium Accrual

    Settlement amount
    Net amount
    Premium
    WHT on Premium
