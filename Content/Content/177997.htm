For SWIFT or Finastra (Misys) Confirmations

NOTES:

  • XML-based message formatting is mandatory for Confirmations in Quantum Six (the older dedicated program methods are no longer supported).
  • The Process confirmations and Match confirmations Operational Settings must be selected before those functions will run.
  • Unless explicitly stated, message types are valid for SWIFT, Electronic, and Finastra (Misys) Confirmations.
  • Quantum Six does not support confirmations for Quotes. The message status for deal quotes will be set to Not Required.

Category 3 - Treasury Markets - Foreign Exchange, Money Market and Derivatives

(TT = Transaction Type)

Format

TT

Used for...

MT300

FX

Foreign Exchange (Currency) Confirmation

MT304

FX

Foreign Exchange Confirmation (used with CLS Continuous Linked Settlement)

(Not Finastra)

MT305

COV

Foreign Currency Option Confirmation (Vanilla plus Asian with PHYSICAL Settlement Type)

With a PHYSICAL Exercise, a separate Confirmation applies for the generated FX Deal (MT300 or MT600 if a Commodity involved).

MT306

COE

Foreign Currency Option Confirmation (Exotic Options except Asian with PHYSICAL Settlement Type)

With a PHYSICAL Exercise, a separate Confirmation applies for the generated FX Deal (MT300 or MT600 if a Commodity involved).

(Not Finastra)

MT320

MM, SE

Fixed Loan/Deposit Confirmation for Money Market Loan/Deposit where underlying Instrument's Formula Timing is:

  • DISCOUNT PREPAID,
  • SIMPLE DISCOUNT PREPAID,
  • YIELD ON MATURITY,
  • YIELD ON STATEMENT, or
  • YIELD - ROLLING.

MT340

(Not Finastra)

FR

(Not Finastra)

(Not Finastra)

(Not Finastra)

Category 5 - Securities Markets

Format

TT

Used for...

MT518

SE, RA

Market-Side Securities Trade Confirmations for Security and Repo deals with stock flows where underlying Instrument Formula Timing is:

  • BOND PERIODIC,
  • CERTIFICATE DEPOSIT PERIODIC,
  • CIB-RBA PERIODIC,
  • DISCOUNT PERIODIC,
  • DISCOUNT PREPAID,
  • FRN-CGL PERIODIC,
  • SIMPLE DISCOUNT PREPAID, or
  • ZERO COUPON DISC -ON MATURITY

(Not Finastra)
