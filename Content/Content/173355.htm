Previous Calculation Points, HR Deal Revaluation

This section displays only when any calculation points have occurred between the Inception Date and Position Date, for effectiveness methods that use Retrospective calculations. For each of these calculation points, the date and cumulative changes in Exposure and Hedge are listed, along with the Effectiveness ratio.

Example

TIP: Calculation points include any Dedesignations.

To determine how the effectiveness percentage was calculated for a specific calculation point

  1. Click on a data item in the calculation point row.

    Another Revaluation Analysis session displays, with the Position Date the same as the calculation date.

  2. Drill down for further details specific to the calculation point.

NOTE: You can use this information to determine exactly how the effectiveness figure at a specific point was calculated. Due to the cumulative nature of the effectiveness figures, the most recent calculation points are used in the current retrospective effectiveness calculations.
