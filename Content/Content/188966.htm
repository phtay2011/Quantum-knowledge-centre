Repos Posting, Off Balance Sheet tab

NOTE: To use these entries, make sure the Apply contingent accounting check box in Accounting Rules is selected.

Entries for Securities Borrowing and Lending Repos

These entries record the borrowing or lending of a particular security, usually against collateral in the form of some other security. The lender retains incidence of ownership, and receives reinvestment interest on coupons and all other benefits that occur during the term of the loan. At maturity of the Repo, the securities are returned at the original principal fee plus a security lending fee.

The available entries are:

  • Switch Exposure Criteria - Records the contingent position for both security borrowing and lending.
  • Switch in Exposure - Credit Balance Sheet account

    Records the contingent exposure for the benefit on a security borrow and the collateral on a security lend.

  • Switch Out Exposure - Debit Balance Sheet account

    Records the contingent exposure for the benefit on a security lend and the collateral on a security borrow

Entries for Buy/Sell Back and Sell/Buy Back

These entries record the forward settling of deals. The entries created will depend on the accounting and accrual methods for the underlying security of these Quantum Repo formats. These are sourced from the Principal Accounting and the Accrual Method settings in Accounting Rules for the Money Market products. This applies to such things as FACE VALUE or NET VALUE Principal Accounting and Capital/Interest Accrual settings.

NOTE: These entries are posted only where the underlying security uses the Bond Periodic formula.

  • Face Value Borrowing Exposure - Debit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the buy of the security. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

    Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the sell of the security. It is reversed on Maturity Date.

    NOTE: If the Principal Accounting field in Accounting Rules is set to Net Value, the Face Value option is not used.

  • Face Value Investment Exposure - Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the sell of the security. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

    Debit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the buy of the security. It is reversed on Maturity Date.

    NOTE: If the Principal Accounting field in Accounting Rules is set to Net Value, the Face Value option is not used.

  • Interest Expense Exposure - Debit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the buy of the security. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

    Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the sell of the security. It is reversed on Maturity Date.

  • Interest Income Exposure - Credit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the sell of the security. It is reversed on Settlement Date.

    Debit Off Balance Sheet account.

    Posted on Deal Date to record the contingent position of the buy of the security. It is reversed on Maturity Date.
