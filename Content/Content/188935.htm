Hedge Relationships Posting Rules

When you have set up the Accounts required for Hedge Accounting use the Hedge Relationships tab to define the rules that determine to which accounts a deal posts G/L entries. You only need to define accounts for those entries that your account setup will post to.

TIP: Before you create or modify a posting rule, ensure you have selected the correct Entity or Accounting Type.

The types of entries created vary from product to product. This section describes the entries created for Hedge Relationships, for:

  • OCI (Other Comprehensive Income)-related Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss accounts*, used by Cash Flow hedges

    Other Comprehensive Income (OCI) is an accounting presentation term for a Balance Sheet account. The account is in the Shareholder Equity section of the Balance Sheet.

    If a Cash Flow hedge is effective, FAS 133 allows you to defer the effective portion of the Mark-to-Market posting from Profit and Loss to OCI. When the amounts are transferred back to the Profit and Loss, the net effect on the Balance Sheet accounts is nil.

    There is no concept of OCI for a Fair Value hedge.

  • MTM (Mark to Market)-related Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss accounts*, used by Fair Value hedges

*TIP: Hedge Accounting does not post to Off Balance Sheet accounts.

OCI-related accounts

Cash flow hedges require the following Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss accounts. In each case, amounts transferred from the Balance Sheet to Profit & Loss impact the associated P&L account.

Balance Sheet (B/S)

Profit & Loss (P&L)

Hedge Fwd Point Balance

Holds the Forward Point balance to be amortized

Hedge Fwd Point Reclass

OCI Balance (amortizing)

Holds the balance of OCI to be amortized

Hedge Reclass (amortizing)

OCI Balance (current)

Holds the ongoing adjustments to OCI during the life of a Hedge Relationship

Hedge Reclass (current)

OCI Balance (Hedging Costs)

Holds the accumulated amount of the option's time value that is aligned to the hedged item.

Hedging Costs Reclass

OCI Reclassification (B/S)

Holds amounts transferred from OCI to B/S

OCI Reclassification (P&L)

Holds amounts reclassified from OCI to P&L

Spot to Spot Balance

Holds the ongoing Spot to Spot Adjustments to offset remeasurement of the underlying exposure at Spot rates, under FAS52.

Spot to Spot Reclass

MTM-related accounts

Fair Value hedges use the Revaluation Gain and Revaluation Loss Balance Sheet accounts to hold the ongoing Mark-to-Market (MTM) adjustments during the life of a Hedge Relationship.

  • Revaluation Gain
  • Revaluation Loss

Amounts transferred from the Balance Sheet to Profit & Loss impact the Gain and Loss P&L accounts.
