Left Side, FX Back to Back deal

This area shows the key values for the first of the two back-to-back FX deals (which is always shown on the left side).

In the header:

  • For a new deal, do not enter a Deal Number in this field. Two unique sequential deal numbers are automatically assigned by Quantum when you save the deal.
  • To access an existing deal, enter the Deal Number (or click button and select from the list). The details of both deals are displayed.
  • Optionally enter a deal Ticket Number for reference purposes.
  • Select an FX Instrument name for the deal. Other left-side fields are defaulted from that instrument, and the rightInstrument field defaults to this one (but you can change it)

On the Deal Details tab, select, enter or edit the left-side deal's key data, as required. The following values default from the left to the right side:

  • Entity,
  • Buy Currency to Sell Currency and vice versa (these cannot be changed),
  • Sell Amount, and
  • Deal Date.

On the Other Details tab, select, enter or edit the options and settings for managing the regulatory reporting and clearing requirements for the left-side deal.

For details of an item, click on it when the Help panel is displayed.
