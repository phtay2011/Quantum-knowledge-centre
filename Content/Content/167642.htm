Relative Date Logic in Analytics Reporting

Analytics supports relative date logic which allows modifiers to be applied to all date fields.

Expressions

The following expressions are acceptable in the form:

Date

Textual date in current regional setting format.

DateKeyword

Today|Query Start Date|EOM|SOM, etc

DateModifier

+|- 0...9|D|W|M|Y|BD|BW|BM|BQ|BY|MBD, etc

DateFunctionName

SOM|EOM, etc

DateFunction

DateFunctionName(DateExpression){DateModifier}

DateExpression

DateFunction|DateKeyword|Date{DateModifier}

Date Expression

DateExpression Date Modifier

Where:

{} = Optional

| = Or

NOTE: The date modifier expressions are case/space insensitive.

Modified Business Day Definitions

Function Abbreviation

Description

BD, BW, BM, BQ, BY

Business day, week, Month, quarter, year.

If the date falls on a non-business day, the date will be moved to the next business day.

MBD

Modified Business Day.

If the date falls on a non-business day, the date will be moved to the next business day.

If the next business day falls into a new month, the date will be rolled to the next available business day of the original month.

MNBD

Modified Next Business Day.

If the date falls on a non-business day, the date will be moved to the next business day irrespective of whether this is the next month.

MPBD

Modified Previous Business Day.

If the date falls on a non-business day, the date will be moved to the previous business day irrespective of whether it moves into the previous month.
