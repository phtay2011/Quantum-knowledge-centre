NOTE:

  • If a price is entered as a fraction, Quantum 'pads' numbers to the right of the point, making them increments of the tick basis. For example, if the tick basis is 32, 123.1 becomes 123.01 (123 and 1/32nd).
  • For a contract entered in decimals, Quantum interprets values as they appear: both 76.5 and 76.50 mean "76 and 50/100ths", 76.05 means "76 and 5/100ths".
