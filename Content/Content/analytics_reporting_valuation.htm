Valuation area

Use this area to specify the following default valuation settings for the report. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, the fields below apply to all report types.

Rule - Select the required Valuation Rule record.

Revaluation Timing - Select one of the following to determine the timing of deals, cash flows, and bank balances to be included in valuations:

  • Start of Day - to include all deals live today (whether dealt today or earlier and whether maturing today or later). If Bank Balances are included in the report, the Start of Day balance is used, with today's cash flows included in deal valuations.
  • Intra Day - to include only deals live today but maturing later. If Bank Balances are included in the report, the Intra Day balance (Start of Day balance + today's cash flows) is used, with today's cash flows excluded from deal valuations.
  • End of Day - to include only deals live today but maturing later, but with the valuation date as the Position Date plus one day, so that overnight accruals are also included.

Currency Conversion - Optionally select a different currency to convert valuations into (otherwise valuations are made in the deal currency).

For Position and Cash Flow queries, the conversion is applied only when the Convert to base currency option is selected.

Duration Weight (Position reports only) - Select Gross PV to exclude, or Net PV to include, the first settlement cash flow from the PV calculation of transaction duration.

Risk Allocation (Position and Cash Flow reports only) - Select one of the following dates for "bucketing" transaction cash flows when a Time Profile is used in a query:

  • Maturity Date - to bucket cash flows according to the Maturity Date of the transaction to which they belong.
  • Repricing Date - to bucket cash flows according to their repricing date (Maturity Date for fixed rate transactions or next Rateset date for floating rate transactions).
  • Cash Flow Date (Cash Flow reports only) - to bucket cash flows according to the cash flow date.

Cash Flow Method (Cash Flow reports only) - Select Repricing, Projected, Projected - exclude floating, or Principal at Risk.

Time Horizon (Parametric and Monte Carlo VaR reports only) - Enter the desired time in days.

Risk Metrics rates are provided for a 1-day or 1-month Time Horizon. If VaR reporting is required for some other Time Horizon, an adjustment is made to the Volatility rates. For example, for a 10-day time horizon, 1-day Volatility rates are multiplied by the square root of 10.

Random Number Seed (Monte Carlo VaR reports only) - enter a starting or seed number to be used in the generation of simulated rates. Using the same Random Number Seed ensures that an identical result can be produced for a Monte Carlo simulation. Changing it will produce a different result.
