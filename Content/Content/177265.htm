Postings (Currency Option Accounting Rules)

This section summarizes the system-generated accounting entries for Rebates, Premiums, and Settlements when the different Accounting Rule settings apply.

Rebates

The accounting entries generated on Knock-out date depend on the date the rebate is settled and the Apply Rebate Accrual setting, as follows:

  • Knock-Out Date = Rebate Date; Apply Rebate Accrualselected:

    On the Knock-Out Date,

    • for a Payable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Rebate Expense Accrual
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Receivable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr P&L Rebate Income Accrual

  • Knock-Out Date = Rebate Date; Apply Rebate Accrualcleared:

    On the Knock-Out Date,

    • for a Payable Rebate:

      Dr P&L Rebate Expense
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Receivable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr P&L Rebate Income

  • Knock-Out Date before Rebate Date; Apply Rebate Accrualselected:

    On the Knock-Out Date,

    • for a Payable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Rebate Expense Accrual
      Cr B/S Rebate Payable

    • for a Receivable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Rebate Receivable
      Cr B/S Rebate Income Accrual

    On the Rebate Payment Date,

    • for a Payable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Rebate Payable
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Receivable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr B/S Rebate Receivable

  • Knock-Out Date before Rebate Date; Apply Rebate Accrualcleared:

    On the Knock-Out Date,

    • for a Payable Rebate:

      Dr P&L Rebate Expense
      Cr B/S Rebate Payable

    • for a Receivable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Rebate Receivable
      Cr P&L Rebate Income

    On the Rebate Payment Date,

    • for a Payable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Rebate Payable
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Receivable Rebate:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr B/S Rebate Receivable

Premiums

The accounting entries generated on Deal date depend on the date the premium is settled and the Accrue Premium setting, as follows:

  • Deal Date = Premium Date; Accrue Premiumselected:

    On the Deal Date,

    • for a Buy option:

      Dr B/S Premium Expense Accrual
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Sell option:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr P&L Premium Income Accrual

  • Deal Date = Premium Date; Accrue Premiumcleared:

    On the Deal Date,

    • for a Buy option:

      Dr P&L Premium Expense
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Sell option:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr P&L Premium Income

  • Deal Date before Premium Date; Accrue Premiumselected:

    On the Deal Date,

    • for a Buy option:

      Dr B/S Premium Expense Accrual
      Cr B/S Premium Payable

    • for a Sell option:

      Dr B/S Premium Receivable
      Cr P&L Premium Income Accrual

    On the Premium Payment Date,

    • for a Buy option:

      Dr B/S Premium Payable
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Sell option:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr B/S Premium Receivable

  • Deal Date before Premium Date; Accrue Premiumcleared:

    On the Deal Date,

    • for a Buy option:

      Dr P&L Premium Expense
      Cr B/S Premium Payable

    • for a Sell option:

      Dr B/S Premium Receivable
      Cr P&L Premium Income

    On the Premium Payment Date,

    • for a Buy option:

      Dr B/S Premium Payable
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for a Sell option:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr B/S Premium Receivable

Cash Settlements

The accounting entries generated on Exercise out date for cash settlement amounts depend on the date the amount is settled, as follows:

  • Exercise Date = Settlement Date:

    On the Value date,

    • for Cash Payable:

      Dr P&L Realized Loss
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for Cash Receivable:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr B/S Realized Gain

  • Exercise Date before Settlement Date:

    On the Exercise date,

    • for Cash Payable:

      Dr P&L Realized Loss
      Cr B/S Settlement Payable

    • for Cash Receivable:

      Dr B/S Settlement Receivable
      Cr P&L Realized Gain

    On the Value date,

    • for Cash Payable:

      Dr B/S Settlement Payable
      Cr B/S Bank Account

    • for Cash Receivable:

      Dr B/S Bank Account
      Cr B/S Settlement Receivable

These entries will be generated and attached to the Currency Option deal when a cash settlement Exercise is performed (in addition to the resulting cash flow).
