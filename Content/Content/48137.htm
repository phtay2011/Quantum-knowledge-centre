End-of-Day Mode

A basic premise in Quantum is that interest on a transaction is earned for the first day, then for each day up until, but not including, the last day.

For interest calculation purposes, End-of-Day mode means that calculations are made assuming the particular day is over - that is, all events for that date are deemed to have been completed - except when the Shift End of Day Curve option in System Defaults (Other Details tab) is cleared (see below).

End-of-Day mode is activated by setting the As At End of Day option in Revaluation Analysis. It relates to Accruals, Currency Translations, and Revaluations (in Position Inquiries), as follows.

Accruals

Always deemed to be in End-of-Day mode

Exchange Fluctuations

Always deemed to be in End-of-Day mode

Revaluations (Position Query)

You can select either Intra-Day or End-of-Day mode.

Accounting postings for revaluations can only be made in End-of-Day mode.

When End-of-Day mode is activated:

  • If the Shift End of Day Curve option is selected, any valuation that relies on a Discount Factor generated from the Zero Curve will have its Days To Maturity adjusted.
  • If the Shift End of Day Curve option is cleared:
    • Any valuation that relies on a Discount Factor generated from the Zero Curve will not have its Days To Maturity adjusted.
    • A Discount Factor generated from the Zero Curve used to NPV a value will not change.
    • Any other factor not calculated from the Zero Curve will have its Days To Maturity adjusted, and therefore will calculate a new valuation factor in each End-of-Day mode.

Differences between Analytics and Quantum

The only difference s that Analytics does not have a Shift End of Day Curve setting. Its End-of-Day behavior is the same asQuantum's when Shift End of Day Curve is selected.
