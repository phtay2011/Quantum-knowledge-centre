Currency Swap Posting, Profit and Loss tab

  • Bond Discount: Pay - Debit Profit and Loss account. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Bond Discount Accrual Pay (B/S) account.
  • Bond Discount: Receive - Credit Profit and Loss account. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Bond Discount Accrual Receive (B/S) account.
  • Bond Premium: Pay - Credit Profit and Loss account. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Bond Premium Accrual Pay (B/S) account.
  • Bond Premium: Receive - Debit Profit and Loss account. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Bond Premium Accrual Receive (B/S) account.
  • Interest Expense - Debit Profit and Loss account. If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:
    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at the time the deal is created, and contras it by a bank account entry. The entry date is the Interest payment date.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to Interest Expense Accrual (B/S).
  • Interest Income - Credit Profit and Loss account. See Interest Expense (P/L).
  • Adhoc Amount - Debit/Credit Profit and Loss account. The Accruals Posting report creates this posting if it is an Adhoc Interest amount and the Adhoc Amount Translation check box in Accounting Rules is selected. Quantum creates the entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry if it is Accrue None or an Adhoc Amount.
  • Gain: Realized - Credit Profit and Loss account.

    If an instrument is unwound, irrespective of the posting method this default records the gain on unwind and is posted at the time of the unwind but dated at the Settlement Date of the unwind.

    If the applicable Revalue deals check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, Quantum does not post transaction revaluations, so this default is not used.
    • Selected, using an NPV-based revaluation method (for example, Bills Net or Projected Net), Quantum posts revaluations. However this default is only used before closeout if Posting method is set to Realized. When this is the case, the Revaluations posting report creates this entry, and contras it to Revaluation Gain: Realized (B/S). On settlement, there is no unrealized amount to reverse.
  • Gain: Unrealized - Credit Profit and Loss account.

    If the applicable Revalue deals check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, Quantum does not post transaction revaluations, so this default is not used.
    • Selected (using Bills or Projected), Quantum posts revaluations.

    If the Posting method is set to:

    • Realized, this default is not used.
    • Unrealized, the Revaluations posting report creates this entry, and contras it to Revaluation Gain: Unrealized (B/S).

    On closeout, Quantum reverses the unrealized amount, and records the realized amount using the Gain: Realized (P/L) or Loss: Realized (P/L) accounts.

  • Loss: Realized - Debit Profit and Loss account. See Gain: Realized (P/L).
  • Loss: Unrealized - Debit Profit and Loss account. See Gain: Unrealized (P/L).
  • CCY Trans Gain Bank: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. If the settlement amount is accrued, this records the difference in the base currency interest amount for non-base currency deals arising as a result of the change in the exchange rates of the accrual entries compared with the settlement interest cash flow.
  • CCY Trans Gain Bank: Unreal - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the unrealized differences between base currency and natural currency interest amounts for non-base currency deals, arising as a result of changes in exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Gain Interest: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. If the settlement amount is accrued, this records the difference in the base currency interest amount for non-base currency deals arising as a result of the change in the exchange rates of the accrual entries compared with the settlement interest cash flow.
  • CCY Trans Gain Interest: Unreal - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the unrealized differences between base currency and natural currency interest amounts for non-base currency deals, arising as a result of changes in exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Gain Principal: Real - Credit Profit and Loss account. If the settlement amount is accrued, this records the difference in the base currency interest amount for non-base currency deals arising as a result of the change in the exchange rates of the accrual entries compared with the settlement principal cash flow.
  • CCY Trans Gain Principal: Unreal - Credit Profit and Loss account. This records the unrealized differences between base currency and natural currency principal amounts for non-base currency deals, arising as a result of changes in exchange rates.
  • CCY Trans Loss Bank: Real - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Bank: Real (P/L).
  • CCY Trans Loss Bask: Unreal - See CCY Trans Gain Bank: Unreal (P/L).
  • CCY Trans Loss Interest: Real - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Interest: Real (P/L).
  • CCY Trans Loss Interest: Unreal - See CCY Trans Gain Interest: Unreal (P/L).
  • CCY Trans Loss Principal: Real - Debit Profit and Loss account. See CCY Trans Gain Principal: Real (P/L).
  • CCY Trans Loss Principal: Unreal - See CCY Trans Gain Principal: Unreal (P/L).
  • FID Paid on Investments - RESERVED
  • WTAX Expense - Debit Profit and Loss account.

    This is used for the Withholding Tax on Interest Expense.

    If the Accrue interest check box on the Accounting Rules tab is:

    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the WTAX Expense Accrual (B/S) account.
  • WTAX on Income - Credit Profit and Loss account.

    This is used for the Withholding Tax on Interest Income.

    If the Accrue interest check box on the Accounting Rules tab is:

    • Cleared, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
    • Selected, Accrual Inquiry creates this entry, and contras it to the WTAX on Income Accrual (B/S) account.
