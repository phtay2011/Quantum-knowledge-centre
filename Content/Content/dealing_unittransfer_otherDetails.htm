Other Details tab, Unit Transfer page

Use this tab to enter or edit additional reference information relating to the source and destination Unit Holdings, one or more Locations to determine business days, and optional text comments.

TIP: Fields are optional unless stated otherwise.

Our References

  • Dealer (mandatory) - the dealer for the transfer; defaults to the Dealer from the Holding Instrument or to the logged-on user (if their User record has the Dealer option selected).
  • Ticket Number - deal ticket information text (up to 20 characters) - for example, a transaction reference for interfacing to other deal capture systems.

Their References

  • Broker Reference (applicable when the destination holding Instrument is Exchange Traded) - discretionary text giving any broker reference information.
  • Broker Dealer (applicable when the destination holding Instrument is Exchange Traded) - enter a broker's dealer or select from the drop-down list of any matching Counterparty Contacts (as determined by Counterparty Contact Rules whose Matching Criteria are met by the Transfer).
  • Counterparty Reference (applicable when the destination holding Instrument is Over the Counter) - discretionary text giving any Counterparty reference information.
  • Counterparty Dealer (applicable when the destination holding Instrument is Over the Counter) - enter a Counterparty dealer or select from the drop-down list of any matching Counterparty Contacts (as determined by Counterparty Contact Rules whose Matching Criteria are met by the Transfer).

Location - one or more locations whose calendars will be used to determine valid business days for the destination holding; the location of the specified destination party (Exchange or Counterparty, depending on the destination holding Instrument's Trading Style). The default is obtained from the destination Unit Holding, which in turn defaults to its Holding Instrument.

Comments - enter or edit any comment text relating to the transfer.
