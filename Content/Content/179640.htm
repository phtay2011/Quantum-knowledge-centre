Mandatory fields (DeActivate and ReActivate)

Only cparty and acc_no are mandatory with the mfDeActivate and mfActivate commands.

Deactivate an account

(QID format)

EXEName=BANKACC
cparty=Allied XYZ Bank
acc_no=FX -1000-1200
$DoMenu=mfDeActivate

(QXT format)

@CASHACCOUNT
AccountNumber=100101010
Bank=ADB Netting
$DEACTIVATE

This action is valid for all account modes (Bank Accounts, Broker Accounts, and Stock Accounts).

Normal validation rules apply, as follows:

  • Where an Account is being deactivated and it is still in use:
    • The account will be deactivated.
    • A validation message will be published to the Message Log showing the first ten instances where the Account is still in use.
  • Where an Account is being deactivated and it is used as a Cash Concentration Account:
    • Account deactivation will not occur.
    • A validation message will be published to the message log stating that the account could not be deactivated.

Reactivate a Deactivated Account

(QID format)

EXEName=BANKACC
cparty=Allied XYZ Bank
acc_no=FX -1000-1200

(QXT format)

@CASHACCOUNT
AccountNumber=100101010
Bank=ADB Netting
$ACTIVATE

This action is valid for all account modes (Bank Accounts, Broker Accounts, and Stock Accounts)
