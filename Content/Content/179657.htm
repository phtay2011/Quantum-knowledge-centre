Importing Account Adjustments and Balance Entries

Importing Bank Account Balance Entries

The Bank Account Balance Entry Importer supports the following QID-format fields.

Mandatory fields

  • $DoMenu (mobalanceentry)
  • deal_dt
  • ccy
  • bank
  • bankacc
  • balance
  • $DoMenu (mfinsert)

Optional fields

  • comments

Example

(QID format)

EXEName=BAADJ
$DoMenu=mobalanceentry
deal_dt=03-Mar-2009
ccy=NZD
bank=ANZ Bank
bankacc=RTS Reporting Account
entity=Transactor - Level 2
comments=Comment on entry
balance=1,000,000.00
$DoMenu=mfinsert

(QXT format)

@AccountAdjustment
$NEW
Bank=My External Bank
BankAccountNumber=11662277
Currency=USD
DealDate=2015-08-22
Entity=My Entity
CurrentAdjustmentMode=Balance entry
$$Balance= 32000.00
$INSERT

Importing Bank Account Adjustments

The Bank Account Adjustments Importer supports the following QID-format fields.

Mandatory fields

  • $DoMenu (moaccountadjustment)
  • deal_dt
  • ccy
  • bank
  • bankacc
  • entity
  • ssrow
  • ssbalance
  • ssamount
  • $DoMenu (mfinsert)

Optional fields

  • import_deal_no
  • ticket_no
  • comments
  • ssglcode

The Bank Account Adjustments QID format includes modes for both Account Adjustments and Balance Entry.

Depending on whether the importation is an Account Adjustment or a Balance Entry, the mandatory and optional fields in the QID file will vary. For example, ticket number applies only to Account Adjustment and balance amount applies only to Balance Entry.

NOTE: If the value of mfnew is used for $DoMenu, the default mode Account Adjustment (moaccountadjustment) will be used.

Examples

Single Line

(QID format)

EXEName=BAADJ
$DoMenu=moaccountadjustment
deal_dt=03-Mar-2009
ccy=AUD
bank=Bank
bankacc=Bank Cash Account
entity=Transactor - Level 2
ssrow=1
ssamount=1,013,093,173.00
ssglcode=105400 - AUD
$DoMenu=mfinsert

Multiple Line

(QID format)

EXEName=BAADJ
$DoMenu=moaccountadjustment
ticket_no=ticket 1
deal_dt=03-Mar-2009
ccy=USD
bank=Bank
bankacc=12345678
entity=Transactor - Level 2
comments=comment
ssrow=1
ssamount=1,000,000.00
ssglcode=142320 - USD
ssrow=2
ssamount=500,000.00
ssglcode=105400 - USD
ssrow=3
ssamount=500,000.00
ssglcode=109200 - USD
$DoMenu=mfinsert

(QXT format)

@AccountAdjustment
*DealNumber=6390
$NEW
TicketNumber=test
Bank=ANZ
AccountName=ANZ Settle
BankAccountNumber=000-1111-12345
Comments=comment
Currency=AUD
DealDate=28-Apr-2015
Entity=Transactor - Level 2
CashFlowMode=Bank Account
CurrentAdjustmentMode=Account Adjustment
$$ADJUSTMENTS 1|123456.0000|1000-1020-00
$$ADJUSTMENTS 2|654321.0000|1000-1021-00
$INSERT
