Settlement Result grid, Money Market Deal

The read-only values in this grid give Settlement information for a Money Market deal based on an Instrument with Formula Tining set to: any ANNUITY or BOND, CERTIFICATE DEPOSIT, CIB-RBA, DISCOUNT or SIMPLE DISCOUNT with PREPAID timing, FRN-CGL, INDEXED BOND, INDEXED GILT, or ZERO COUPON DISCOUNT.

Possible grid items are listed below. Their display varies depending on the Formula, as indicated in each case.

The PPH column:

  • applies to Price-based instruments only, and
  • shows the PPH (price per hundred) rounding of the Capital, Income, and Settlement prices (when they appear).

For an Indexed Bond Instrument, the values are index-adjusted.

Knowledge Center

Index

(Displayed for CIB-RBA, INDEXED BOND, and INDEXED GILT instruments.)

The indexed (inflation-adjusted) component of the settlement price of a bond. This represents the difference between the Face Value and the Indexed Face Value.

Knowledge Center

Face Value

(Displayed for FRN-CGL instruments with Asset Backed selected.)

The principal value of the transaction, as entered on this tab.

Knowledge Center

Premium / Discount

(Displayed for any BOND, CERTIFICATE DEPOSIT, CIB-RBA, FRN-CGL, INDEXED GILT, or ZERO COUPON DISCOUNT Instrument.)

The premium or discount component of the settlement price of a bond. This represents the difference between the present value of the coupon stream and the dealt market yield.

  • For an Indexed Bond Instrument, this value is index-adjusted.
  • For a Bond/Periodic or FRN instrument with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option set, any Withholding Tax (WHT) amount will be displayed in the associated Disc WHT or Prem WHT field, as applicable.

    TIP: The WHT rate is that specified in the Taxes box on the Other Details tab, which defaults to that specified for the Counterparty or Entity, depending on the Buy/Sell setting.

Capital and PPH

(Displayed for any BOND, CERTIFICATE DEPOSIT, CIB-RBA, FRN-CGL, INDEXED GILT, or ZERO COUPON DISCOUNT Instrument.)

The capital component of the settlement price of the bond. This represents the sum of the Face Value and the Discount or Premium.

The PPH column shows the Price Per Hundred rounding of the Capital price.

For an Indexed Bond Instrument, these values are index-adjusted.

Interest

(Displayed for DISCOUNT PREPAID or SIMPLE DISCOUNT PREPAID instruments.)

The interest component of the Settlement Price.

Income and PPH

(Displayed for any ANNUITY or BOND, CERTIFICATE DEPOSIT, CIB-RBA, FRN-CGL, or INDEXED GILT instruments.)

The net coupon component of the Settlement Price; the difference between the Settlement and Capital values, less any WHT amount.

  • WHT (Withholding Tax) applies only to BOND PERIODIC (non-callable) or FRN (not Asset-backed) instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option set, AND when a WHT rate is specified. The WHT amount (if any) is displayed in either the Disc WHT or Prem WHT field, as applicable, and the WHT rate is that specified for the deal in the Taxes field on the Other Details tab. This rate defaults to that specified for the Counterparty or Entity, depending on the Buy/Sell setting.
  • The coupon amount can differ, depending is whether the coupon is Cum or Ex.
  • For a Floating Rate instrument (FRN) with more than one Rateset prior to the Settlement Date, the system will assume - at deal save - that rates have been constant since the start of the coupon period up to the Settlement Date, whether or not there has been more than one rateset prior to that date. In that situation, you will usually need to change the Income value to reflect the correct value (if you do, note that the Settlement Amount on the Cash Flows tab will not reflect the new settlement value until after you save the deal).

The PPH column shows the Price Per Hundred rounding of the Income component.

For an Indexed Bond Instrument, both values are index-adjusted.

Settlement and PPH

(Displayed for any ANNUITY or BOND, CERTIFICATE DEPOSIT, CIB-RBA, DISCOUNT or SIMPLE DISCOUNT with PREPAID timing, FRN-CGL, INDEXED GILT, or ZERO COUPON DISCOUNT instruments.)

The settlement price or value:

  • for Discounted deals - the settlement value; the net of the Face Value and the Interest amounts, or
  • for Bond-style or FRN deals - the settlement price of the bond: Capital + Income - any WHT* amount.

    *NOTE: WHT (Withholding Tax) applies only to BOND PERIODIC (non-callable) or FRN (not Asset-backed) instruments with the Capital Discount/Premium WHT option set, AND when a WHT rate is specified. The WHT amount (if any) is displayed in either the Disc WHT or Prem WHT field, as applicable.

    The WHT rate is that specified for the deal in the Taxes field on the Other Details tab. This rate defaults to that specified for the Counterparty or Entity, depending on the Buy/Sellsetting.

The PPH column shows the Price Per Hundred rounding of the Settlement value.

For an Indexed Bond Instrument, both values are index-adjusted.
