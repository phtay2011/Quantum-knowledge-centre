BP Delta (Point in Time Column Formulas)

(Position or Cash Flow queries only.)

The value change for a user-defined shift in interest rates. Par rates, Zero rates or an Internal rate of return (IRR) may be shifted, or a differential calculus method of calculation may be used.

The magnitude of the shift (number of Basis Points) is user-defined and can be applied on an annual, semi-annual, or quarterly, basis.
