Fees

Use the Fee Deal page to create and maintain parameters for applying fees to Quantum Money Market, Securities, and/or Interest Rate Swap deals only.

TIP: For a summary of all deal types that may have fees applied, see Implementing Fees on Quantum deals.

Unlike "standard" deal pages, the Fee deal page:

  • uses its own set of system-assigned Deal Numbers, distinct from those across all "standard" deals;
  • has no Deal Map, Cash Flows, nor G/L Entries tabs (because none of those apply). Fee deal records provide settings and schedules for the generation - via the Process Fees page - of fee cash flows and G/L entries (in Actual Cash Flow deals); and
  • is not subject to Deal Approval.

Use the Fee Deal page's:

  • header area - to specify parameters for selecting deals the fee will apply to, and the Fee Name (used for linking and reporting purposes);
  • Calculation tab - to enter at least one Base Template and/or Formula for determining the fee Base Amount and calculating fee values (if more than one, each must cover a unique, non-overlapping date range);
  • Other Details tab - to specify settings for generated fee cash flows (Instrument, Counterparty, Bank Account, Location, and so on) and Fee Estimation Parameters (when applying True-Ups);
  • Accounting tab - to enter Chart of Account codes for Debit and Credit G/L entries required on accrual, invoice, calculation, and payment dates; and
  • Schedule tab - to specify scheduled dates for fee calculation, fee payment, True Up invoicing, and fee base amount resets, and options to:
    • specify whether your schedule is to be based on Calculation dates or Payment dates,
    • suppress fee cash flow processing,
    • pay fee cash flows in advance (Calculation-based schedule only), and
    • suppress true up cash flow processing.

From this page you can also access the other fees-related pages, namely the Fee Base Template, Fee Formula, and Process Fees pages.
