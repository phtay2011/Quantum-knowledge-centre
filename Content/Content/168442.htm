Netting Functions in Settlement Processing

NOTE:

  • A limit on the maximum number of cash flows that may be netted may apply, depending on the NetItemsMax setting in the Web.AppSettings.config file.
  • The only available actions on flows currently Proposed for Netting are either Net as Proposed or Clear Net Proposal.

Net as Proposed

(Button and grid menu; available when one or more groups of cash flows proposed for netting are selected, with or without existing netted groups that they may be appended to.)

Creates one or more netted groups as proposed, appending to existing groups if any selected and proposed; removes Net Proposal numbers and shows new Net Number values as proposed.

Clear Net Proposal

(Grid menu only; available when one or more groups of cash flows proposed for netting are selected.)

Removes Net Proposal numbers, making the selected cash flows independent. (They can then be considered for manual netting using either Create New Net or Append to Net.)

Net

(Button and grid menu; available when two or more cash flows meeting the criteria for netting into a new group are selected.)

Creates one new netted group with a new Net Number.

NOTE: If your selected flows have different Payment Methods, Bank Accounts, and/or Counterparty Accounts, a dialog box will be displayed indicating the differences, and giving you the option to select which Payment Method, Bank Account, and/or Counterparty Account is to apply to the netted group as a whole, or to cancel. For more details, see Netting Flows with Different Accounts/Payment Methods.

Append to Net

(Button and grid menu; available when one existing netted group plus other cash flows meeting the criteria for netting into that group are selected.)

Expands the netted group to include the selected additional cash flows.

NOTE: If your selected flows have different Payment Methods, Bank Accounts, and/or Counterparty Accounts from the existing netted group, a dialog box will be displayed indicating the differences, and giving you the option to select which Payment Method, Bank Account, and/or Counterparty Account is to apply to the expanded netted group, or to cancel. For more details, see Netting Flows with Different Accounts/Payment Methods.

Undo Net

(Grid menu only; available when one or more existing netted groups are selected.)

Dissolves (un-nets) the netted groups entirely, making their cash flows independent; removes the Net Numbers.

TIP: Individual cash flows can be removed from a netted group by using the Net Details function.

Net Details

(Quick menu only; available on a Net group item.)

Opens the Net Details dialog box, which:

  • lists details of all cash flows in the netted group (Deal Number, Ticket Number, Amount, Counterparty, Entity, Transaction Type, Instrument),
  • enables you to optionally remove individual cash flows from the netted group by selecting them and clicking Remove from net, and
  • for a Proposed Net - enables you to optionally action the net by clicking Action Net.
