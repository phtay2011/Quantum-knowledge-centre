Indexed CIB-RBA formulas

The following formulas use Australian Financial Markets Association (AFMA) conventions. They are therefore intended for use with Australian Dollar instruments.

The Capital Indexed Bond - Reserve Bank of Australia (CIB-RBA) pricing formula is a variation of the formula contained in the Commonwealth of Australia Treasury Indexed Bonds Prospectus. It is a formula that can be specified for an Australian Dollar Money Market Security investment based on the Commonwealth of Australia Treasury Indexed Bond Prospectus.

NOTE: A minor alteration has been made to the definition of K (the nominal value of the principal). The K factors used for the Capital formula are derived from the issue date of the Index series.

Final period pricing

Pricing in the final period for instruments using the CIB-RBA formula is defined in the Final Period Pricing section of the Pricing tab on the Money Market or Securities instrument page.
