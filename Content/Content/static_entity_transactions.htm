Transactions tab, Entity

Use this tab to specify, for the entity:

  • Taxes Applicable (optional) the taxes for which the Entity may be liable. These are the default taxes for that Entity's deals. Select from the drop-down list of Taxes available on your system.
  • Receiving interest or Paying interest (under Calculate WTAX based on the Party) - select which party's tax rate determines the default Withholding Tax calculation method for the entity's Money Market, Securities, and Interest Rate Swap deals
  • Country
  • Non Business Days Location
  • Exposure (Trading) or Cover (Hedging) (under Reporting Classification) - select whether the transactions for this entity are Trading or Hedging, respectively (for interest and currency position reporting purposes)
  • Include Dealer in closeout matching criteria - select this option to include the Dealer as one of the criteria for closeouts for this entity
  • Entity is a SWIFT Financial Institution - select this option to identify the entity as a SWIFT financial institution for EFT processing purposes
  • Exempt from Clearing - select this option to exclude the entity from having any of its trades cleared through a clearing house
