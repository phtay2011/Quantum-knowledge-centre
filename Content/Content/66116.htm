Accrued interest calculation, ACTUAL/365 (ISDA)

For accrued interest calculations, Equal and Exact coupon calculation results are the same.

Regular coupon

In this example:

Equal

Exact

(As for Exact coupon)

 

Accrued interest calculation: 15 Aug (non-Leap Year) - 23 Jan (Leap Year):

10%*((139/365)+(22/366)) = 4.40931207%, where:

  • 10% = Coupon interest rate
  • 139 = Number of days 15 Aug - 31 Dec (non-Leap Year)
  • 22 = Number of days 1 Jan - 23 Jan (Leap Year)
