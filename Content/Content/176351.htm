G/L postings for OCI dedesignation

The following General Ledger entries are created when dedesignation causes OCI to be released.

The accounting treatment differs depending on:

  • the Hedge Dedesignation rule specified on the Hedge Relationship page's Deal Details tab (AMORTIZED or DEFERRED),

    OR (for a manual dedesignation)

  • the Accounting treatment options specified on the Dedesignation form (Full or Partial).

    If the deal is a HEDGE, this area is read-only, with either Amortize Release or Defer Release set, depending on the Hedge Dedesignation setting on the Deal Details tab.

    If the Deal is an EXPOSURE or HYPOTHETICAL, you can accept the default of Amortize Release or Defer Release (depending on the Hedge Dedesignation setting), OR select Immediate along with an Amount option and (Cashflow Hedges only) an Account option. For more details, see Accounting Treatment under Full Deal Dedesignation form or Partial Deal Dedesignation form.

The G/L postings and their timing are determined by the above setting/s, as below.

In all cases:

On the effective date of the dedesignation, there are postings to:

B/S - OCI Balance (current)
P&L - Hedge Reclass (current)

...and to the amortizing/deferred account equivalents:

B/S - OCI Balance (amort)
P&L - Hedge Reclass (amort)

For an AMORTIZED or DEFERRED dedesignation:

The following posting is also made at the times listed below:

B/S - OCI Balance (amort)
P&L - Hedge Reclass (amort)

...on either:

  • the deferral/accrual/Accounting End date (for a DEFERRED dedesignation), or...
  • the individual amortizing dates up to the Accounting End date(for an AMORTIZED dedesignation)

For an IMMEDIATE dedesignation:

The following posting is also made on the effective date of the dedesignation, and the accounts are determined by the Accounting Treatment Account option selected on the Dedesignation form:

  • Release OCI to Hedge Gain Loss

    B/S - OCI Balance (amort)
    P&L - Hedge Reclass (amort)

  • Reclassify OCI to Profit & Loss

    B/S - OCI Balance (amort)
    P&L - OCI Reclassification (P&L)

  • Reclassify OCI to Balance Sheet

    B/S - OCI Balance (amort)
    P&L - OCI Reclassification (B/S)
