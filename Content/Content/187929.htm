Next Approver - Display or Filter Values in Report Views

In Deal Approval and Settlement Approval, you can now configure report result Views to:

  • display Next Approver as a column in the results grid, or
  • filter on Next Approver, so that only items pending your approval are listed (not those pending first approval you are the second Approver).

    TIP: You could make such a filtered View your default View, so that items you cannot action at that time are not shown by default.

Implementation

When you configure report result Views, you can optionally:

  • select Next Approver in the Columns multi-select field on the Layout tab, or
  • add Next Approver as a Filter parameter on the Filters tab, setting it to Yes or No*.

    *NOTE: Setting the filter value to No will list only items you cannot action at that time.

When you access the Deal Approval or Settlement Approval page, the Next Approver value for each listed item depends on:

  • the respective Deal Approval or Settlement Approval settings in your Approver record (Signature List in particular),
  • the applicable Deal or Settlement Approval and Authorization Rule settings for each item (when 1st Approver and/or 2nd Approver are set to ALL or include your Signature List in each case), and
  • the item's current first and second Approval status (Pending or Approved).
