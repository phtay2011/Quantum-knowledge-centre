Settlement of Deals including Stock Flows

Quantum Settlement Approval grid includes the following features for settlement of stock flows.

NOTE: At deal Maturity the stock flow is no longer linked to the cash flow. Quantum does not support Maturity Confirmations and the flow cannot be Actioned. The cash flows can be handled independently.

  • Linked stock flows and cash flows are grouped together.
  • Where a stock flow has one or more linked cash flows, actioning any one flow in such a linked "package" actions all flows in that package.
  • With a stock flow, only the following actions are supported:
    • Approve
    • Reject
    • Our Instructions
    • Counterparty Instructions
  • When you select a stock flow and view Our Instructions, you can change only the Entity IdentifierPayment Method and SWIFT MT Format values, and (except for Stock Account Transfer flows) the Account Number.

    Changes you make to Payment Method or SWIFT MT Format here for a Stock Transfer, will be reflected in those values on the Settlement Details tab for that Stock Transfer deal - in either the Source or Destination areas, depending on whether the stock is being transferred from or to our Custodian account, respectively.

  • When you select a stock flow and view Counterparty Instructions, you can change the Counterparty Custodian Account Number only. You can select only from Custodian Counterparties.

    If you change the Account Number here for a Stock Account Transfer, the Custodian Settlement Detail on the Settlement Details tab for that Stock Transfer deal changes accordingly- in either the Source or Destination panel, depending on whether the stock is being transferred from or to the Counterparty Custodian account, respectively.

Validation for Stock Flows

You must specify a SWIFT MT Format file and an Entity Identifier before you can action a stock flow.

If you change the Custodian in Counterparty Stock Accounts, a warning message alerts you that it may be necessary to select an alternative SWIFT MT Format Template in Our Instructions.

Financial Changes to the Deal after EFT Processing

If a financial change is made to the deal after the stock flow receipt or delivery message has been sent by the EFT Interface program, Cancelation flows and new flows will appear, and:

  • Actioning the Stock Face Value Reversal flow and running SWIFT EFT will then send the Cancelation message.
  • Actioning the New Stock Face Value flow and running SWIFT EFT will then send the New Stockflow message.

If the deal is deleted after the stock flow message has been sent, only Cancelation flows will appear.

Changing a stock flow after EFT Processing

In Settlements, once the stock flow receipt or delivery message has been sent, you cannot change anything on the stock flow. Change the Deal details instead.
