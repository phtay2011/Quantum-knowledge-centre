Prevent FX Deal Requests from Replicating Into Analytics

(First introduced in Release 6.6.1 SP, March 2018.)

You can now prevent Quantum FX Deal Requests from being automatically replicated into Analytics.

Implementation

A new custom setting has been added to the Quantum Configuration tool for Analytics Sites, to prevent FX Deal Requests from automatically replicating (via RTU or manual) from Quantum to Analytics.

To enable the blocking of Deal Requests to Analytics

  1. On the Configure Site Sections page, select Advanced on the Analytics tab when editing you Site section.

  2. On the Advanced Settings form, click Custom Settings.
  3. In the Custom Setting field, enter "BLOCKREQUESTS"; click Add and then click OK to save the setting.

  4. On the Advanced Settings form, enter "YES" in the BLOCKREQUESTS field that is now available.

NOTES:

  • Money Market Deal Requests are not replicated to Analytics, regardless of the BLOCKREQUESTS setting.
  • FX Deal Requests are replicated to Analytics by default, unless you set BLOCKREQUESTS to YES.
  • Once an FX request has been filled then the resultant FX deal should flow from Quantum to Analytics, regardless of whether you set BLOCKREQUESTS to YES or NO.

History

Previously, FX Deal Requests, created in Quantum, were automatically replicated to Analytics, which affected deal reporting in Analytics.
