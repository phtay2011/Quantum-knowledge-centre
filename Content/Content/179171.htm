Amount (Futures Accounting Rule)

(Available only when Apply from deal date to settlement date is selected.)

Select which calculated amount is to be posted for contingent entries - one of the following:

  • NOMINAL - to calculate contingent entries as Face value* (nominal value) multiplied by the number of Lots*, post on deal date, and reverse on closeout match date, exercise date, or settlement date, whichever occurs first.

    *TIPS:

    • Face Value is as entered in the Lots Details panel of the relevant Futures or ETO Instrument page.
    • Lots is as entered on the Deal Details tab of the ETO dealing page.
  • SETTLEMENT - to calculate contingent entries based on the notional settlement value of the contract, as follows:
    • for yield-based contracts - the deal or strike price settlement value, or
    • for fixed tick contracts:

      (Deal or Strike price/tick size in decimals) * Tick Value * Price * Lots.

    These entries are posted on deal date and reversed on closeout match date, exercise date, or the contract settlement date, whichever occurs first.
