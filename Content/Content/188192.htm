Overnight Index Swap Instruments

Overnight Index Swap (OIS) deals have the following characteristics:

On business days...

On non-business days...

A floating interest rate is rateset daily.

There are no ratesets.

Interest is accrued and compounded daily.

Simple interest is accrued on the preceding business day on a Straight Line basis.

If the deal Maturity Date is a non-business day, the deal attracts interest up to the following business day.
