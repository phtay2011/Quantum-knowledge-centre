Repos with South African Pricing

On the Repo instrument page, the Apply South African pricing option is available when Repo Type is Buy/Sell Back, Sell/Buy Back, Classic, or Reverse, as highlighted in the example below.

This option:

  • is cleared by default,
  • is cleared for all pre-existing Repo instruments following an upgrade to this release,
  • may be changed on any existing or new Classic, Reverse, Buy/Sell Back, or Sell/Buy Back Repo instrument, and
  • when selected:
    • the selected Repo Currency should be South African (ZAR),
    • the selected Collateral Instrument should specify the BOND PERIODIC formula and the South African default currency (ZAR), and
    • the Pay back coupon option is unavailable (because coupons are not paid back for this Repo type in the South African market).

On the Repo Dealing page when the selected Instrument is one with the Apply South African pricing option selected:

  • the Pay back coupon option on the dealing page is cleared and disabled,
  • pricing and Deal Map generation apply the South African Repo Pricing formula, and therefore...
  • when the remaining deal details are populated, the adjusted collateral information is displayed, including Settlement and Yield.

  • A Repo deal saved with South African pricing may be rolled forward (renegotiated) and the calculation of interest and settlement remains consistent with the South African model.
  • The same pricing will be applied whether deals are entered via the Repo Dealing page or the Deal Importer.
