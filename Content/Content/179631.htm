NOTES:

  1. Do not use Carriage Return/Line Feed in the Comments field. For multi-line comments, use the pipe character | to represent a new display line. For example, the following import file content:

    comments=Multi-line comment:|Second Line|Third line.

    ...would appear in the Comments text box on the Money Market Deals form as:

    Multi-line comment:
    Second Line
    Third line.

  2. The p_pay_receive_type and r_pay_receive_type names refer to the Constant Maturity check boxes on the Pay side and Receive side, respectively.
  3. The p_review_1 and r_review_1 fields do not default in Import mode and must therefore be explicitly specified.
