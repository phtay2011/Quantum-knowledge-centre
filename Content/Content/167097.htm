Double Barrier Currency Options Formula (Sidenius)

European Double Barrier options are valued using a closed-form approximation to the model derived by Sidenius (1998). This model is suitable for options with a double knock-out barrier or a double knock-in barrier. For the former, the option is knocked out if the spot rate breaches the barrier from below (up-and-out) or from above (down-and-out). For the latter, the option is knocked in if the spot rate breaches the barrier from below (up-and-in) or from above (down-and-in).

Explicit formulas are required only for the double knock-out options. Knock-in options can be valued as a combination of a Vanilla option (valued using Garman-Kolhagen) and a double knock-out barrier option.
