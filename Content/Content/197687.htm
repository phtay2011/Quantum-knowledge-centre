Negative Interest Rate General Ledgers

Money Market & Securities

In 2019.1 (6.8.2), we added Negative Interest Income and Negative Interest Expense accounting tags to Rules > Posting > Money Market & Securities.

  • In the Balance Sheet tab:
    • Negative Interest Expense Accrual
    • Negative Interest Income Accrual
  • In the Profit and Loss tab:
    • Negative Interest Expense
    • Negative Interest Expense

When you save a negative rate Money Market (MM) deal, interest postings now use a separate account associated with negative interest rates. When you later run Accruals for the same deals, accrual postings use a separate account associated with the negative interest rates.

  • Accruals on - Interest postings post to Negative Interest Expense Accrual (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income Accrual (investment deals)
  • Accruals off - Interest postings post to Negative Interest Expense (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income (investment deals)

General Ledger (GL) entries for the INTEREST deal map item and accrual entries post to different accounts based on whether the rate is positive or negative.

Affected, and exceptions

MM instruments affected by this change are:

  • Yield Periodic
  • Yield on Maturity
  • Yield On Statement
  • Yield Rolling
  • Yield CDI Periodic
  • Yield CDI Rolling
  • Yield Compound Periodic
  • Yield Compound on Maturity
  • Yield Compound Rolling
  • Discount Periodic
  • Discount Prepaid

Excluded from 2019.4 (6.8.2) – MM deals that use a Floating instrument and where Rate Set Frequency is greater than Payment Frequency (i.e. rates are set more often than interest is paid/received). In the case of these deals, interest and accruals are posted on Positive tags as before.

To use current functionality

If you do not want to use the new functionality, use the supplied upgrade script to update the new negative posting tags to the values of the corresponding positive account tags. At a later time, if you want to you can use the script to update your site to use negative tags.

Example: A negative rate MM deal, accruals off

When you save a negative rate MM deal with the instrument Discount Prepaid, then Discount Amount Postings use a separate account related to the negative interest rate.

GL entries for the DISCOUNT AMOUNT deal map item post to different accounts based on whether the rate is positive or negative:

  • Accruals on - Discount postings post to Negative Interest Expense Accrual (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income Accrual (investment deals)
  • Accruals off - Discount postings post to Negative Interest Expense (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income (investment deals)

The example shows a MM deal with accruals turned off, Discount Prepaid Instrument (always Fixed), with a negative interest rate.

Unwind

When you set up a partial or full unwind on a MM deal with a negative interest rate, unwind postings clear the Negative Interest Rate Account.

GL entries for the UNWIND deal map item post to different accounts based on whether the rate is positive or negative:

  • Accruals on - Unwind postings post to Negative Interest Expense Accrual (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income Accrual (investment deals)
  • Accruals off - Unwind postings post to Negative Interest Expense (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income (investment deals)

Affected

MM instruments affected are:

  • Yield Periodic
  • Yield on Maturity
  • Yield Rolling
  • Yield CDI Periodic
  • Yield Compound Periodic
  • Yield Compound on Maturity
  • Discount Periodic
  • Discount Prepaid

Example: A negative rate MM deal, accruals on, unwound

This example shows a MM deal with accruals turned on, based on a Yield Periodic, Floating instrument, with negative interest rate and one rate set with positive interest. The partial unwind happened in a period with a negative interest rate; a full unwind afterwards in a period with a positive interest rate.

Capitalize interest

If you use the Capitalize Interest function on a negative rate, Capitalize Interest postings clear the Negative Interest Account to the Principal Account.

GL entries for the CAPITALIZE INTEREST deal map item post to different accounts based on whether the rate is positive or negative.

  • Accruals on - Capitalize Interest postings post to Negative Interest Expense Accrual (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income Accrual (investment deals)
  • Accruals off - Capitalize Interest postings post to Negative Interest Expense (borrowing deals) or Negative Interest Income (investment deals)

Affected, and exceptions

MM instrument affected is:

  • Yield Periodic

For the three instruments listed below, the change is not applied in 2019.1 (6.8.2) since Capitalization of Interest is done via the deal map and then the system applies the deal status Customized. For those deals, after capitalization is done, both Interest and capitalization items are posted to Positive GL Accounts, even when interest rate on deal is negative.

  • Yield Rolling
  • Yield CDI Rolling
  • Yield Compound Rolling

NOTE: Any MM deal that uses WTAX are not included in 2019.4 (6.8.2) - all INTEREST, ACCRUAL, UNWIND and CAPITALIZE GL entries are posted against Positive GL Accounts as before.

Interest Rate Swaps

In 2019.1 (6.8.2), we added Negative Interest Income and Negative Interest Expense accounting tags to the Static > Posting Rule > Interest Rate Swaps tab.

In the Balance Sheet:tab:

  • Negative Interest Income Accrual
  • Negative Interest Expense Accrual

In the Profit & Loss tab:

  • Negative Interest Income
  • Negative Interest Expense

When you save an Interest Rate Swap deal with negative interest:

  • Receive leg of an Interest Rate Swap - The system posts against the Negative Interest Income Posting Rule tag
  • Pay leg of an Interest Rate Swap - The system posts against the Negative Interest Expense Posting Rule tag

Affected instructions, and exceptions

Interest Rate Swap instruments affected by this change are:

  • Yield Periodic
  • Yield CDI on Maturity
  • Yield Compound on Maturity
  • Yield Compound Periodic
  • Discount Periodic
  • Discount on Maturity
  • Simple Discount on Maturity
  • Bond Periodic

Excluded from 6.8.2 are Floating IRS deals where Rate Set Frequency is greater than Payment Frequency. In this case, interest and accruals are posted to Positive Accounting Tags.

Example: A negative rate IRS deal Instrument, accruals off

The example shows an IRS deal based on a Yield Periodic instrument, with a negative interest rate and accruals turned off.

Pay leg interest when the rate is negative and accruals turned off posts to Negative Interest Expense

Receive leg interest when the rate is negative and accruals turned off posts to Negative Interest Income

Accruals

When generating accruals for Interest Rate Swap deals, the system selects the Posting Rule Account tags based on the leg and the sign of the accrual amount.

  • Receive leg with Negative Accrual Amount - The system posts to Negative Interest Income Account Tags
  • Pay leg with Positive Accrual Amount – The system posts to Negative Interest Expense Account Tags

Example: A negative rate IRS deal, accruals on

This example shows an IRS deal with accruals turned on, based on a Yield Periodic instrument, with negative interest rate.

  • Pay leg interest with negative rate and accruals are turned on - The system posts to Negative Interest Expense Accrual
  • Receive leg interest with negative rate and accruals are turned on - The system posts to Negative Interest Income Accrual

Pay leg accruals post to Negative Interest Expense Account Tags:

  • Dr Negative Interest Expense Accrual
  • Cr Negative Interest Expense

Receive leg accruals post to Negative Interest Income Account tags:

  • Dr Negative Interest Income
  • Cr Negative Interest Income Accrual

IRS Deal Actions – unwind and rate set

Deal Actions that affect IRS interest include Rate Set and Unwind.

The system posts interest to separate accounts based on whether the rate is positive or negative. So when you change the rate from positive to negative by using a Rate Set action, the system will properly post respective interest to negative GL Accounts.

Exclusions

  • Performing multiple Rate Set where Rate Set Frequency is greater than Payment Frequency is excluded from release 6.8.2.
  • IRS deals that use WTAX are excluded from release 6.8.2. Respective GL entries have been posted against Positive GL Accounts.
