About the Unwind Dialog Box

NOTE: The default values for each field on this form are set up to unwind the entire current face value of the deal.

Ticket Number

Enter a ticket number for the Unwind, if appropriate. The ticket number is optional information about the deal, for example a transaction reference for interfacing to other deal capture systems.

This information is stored with the Unwind action and is independent of the Ticket Number for the deal.

Unwind Date

The effective date of the Unwind - that is, the date on which all Unwind calculations are based. This date must be:

  • on or after the deal Settlement Date, and
  • before the deal Maturity Date.

Settlement Date

The date on which the Unwind cash flow is to be settled.

Payment Type

The direction of the Unwind settlement cash flow: Pay or Receive. The default setting is based on whether the deal is a Borrowing (Pay) or Investment (Receive).

Unwind Face Value

Enter the amount of Principal or Face Value to be unwound from the deal.

The default is the total Face Value remaining on the deal. To fully Unwind the deal, accept the default amount.

Currency

(Read-only.)

The currency in which the Unwind settlement amount is stated - that is, the deal currency.

Settlement Amount

The Unwind settlement cash flow amount; the amount that is posted to the bank account on the Unwind Date. Enter a positive number, and ensure Payment Type gives the direction of the settlement.

You can choose whether the cash flow is for the Unwind Face Value only or the Unwind Face Value plus accrued interest and any associated fees, penalties, or charges.

  • Generally, you would enter the Unwind Face Value plus accrued interest and any fees, penalties, or other charges associated with the Unwind. After processing the Unwind, this total settlement amount appears against the Unwind Date on the Cash Flows tab and on the G/L Entries tab as the amount posted to the bank account. The G/L Entries tab also displays the calculated gain or loss posted to the relevant Gain/Loss account.
  • If you enter the Face Value of the Unwind only, the amount that will be shown on the Cash Flows tab for the Unwind Date will not include the accrued interest or other fees and penalties associated with the Unwind. Those amounts are posted to the relevant gain or loss account for that date, and appear only on the G/L Entries tab.

The system calculates and displays the Book Value of the portion of Principal or Face Value being unwound and the Realised Gain/(Loss) that will be posted for the Unwind gain or loss (the difference between the Book Value and the Settlement Amount).

The Book Value amount is broken down into Accrued Interest, Accrued Capital, and/or Accrued WTax components, depending on the type of deal being unwound.

Compound Rate / Average Rate

For deals using overnight index functionality, for example, Dealing > Money Market, find Average Rate by opening the deal and in the menu, select Unwind. Check that the value is present and realistic.

For swaps using overnight index functionality, find Compound Rate - Pay / Compound Rate - Receive by opening Dealing > Interest Rate Swap or Dealing > Cross Currency Swap and, in the page menu, selecting Unwind. Check that the value is present and realistic.

The fields are available in the Unwind dialog box when Formula Timing = OVERNIGHT INDEX PERIODIC for the relevant leg of the interest rate swap instrument.

Analytics calculates the average rate or compound rate from the start of the interest period that the unwind takes place up to the unwind date and populates the field.

  • If you change the unwind date, the system updates the settlement date.
  • You can rollback and unwind.
  • You cannot rate-set a fully unwound deal.

When both Payment Date Offset and Suspension are greater than zero in a Yield Periodic Overnight Index Money Market deal, the following logic is applied:

  • If the unwind effective date is after the penultimate interest effective date - when calling Analytics for the average rate, the non-zero suspension is passed.
  • If the unwind effective date is before the penultimate interest effective date - when calling Analytics for the average rate, the suspension is zero.
  • Analytics is not called when effective date is on interest effective date.

Residual Face Value

(Read-only.)

The Face Value that will remain on the deal after the Unwind is processed.

Accrued Interest

(Read-only.)

The amount of accrued interest payable on the Unwind Face Value on the Unwind Date.

Accrued Interest Currency

(Read-only.)

The currency of the Accrued Interest amount.

Accrued WTax

(Read-only.)

The amount of accrued Withholding Tax payable (if any) on the Unwind Face Value.

Accrued Interest WTax

(Read-only.)

The amount of accrued Withholding Tax payable (if any) on the Accrued Interest amount.

Accrued Capital

(Read-only.)

The amount of accrued Capital.

Accrued Capital WTax

(Read-only.)

The amount of accrued Withholding Tax payable (if any) on the Accrued Capital amount.

Capital Amount

(Read-only.)

The current capital value of the portion of Face Value that you are unwinding.

Book Value

(Read-only.)

The book value of the Unwind Face Value on Settlement Date.

Realised Gain/(Loss)

(Read-only.)

The difference between the Book Value and the Settlement Amount.

Valuation button

Optionally click this button to preview the revaluation effects of the Unwind.

The Deal Revaluation page will opens in a separate window, and you can then run a query to see the results of the Unwind before accepting it.

OK button

Click this button to action the Unwind and close the Unwind dialog box.

Cancel button

Click this button to cancel the Unwind and close the Unwind dialog box.
