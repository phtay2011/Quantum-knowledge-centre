Deal Details tab, Letter of Credit

Use this tab to enter or edit the main details for the Letter of Credit (LC) deal, noting the following:

TIP: Typing a single character in a lookup field sets it to the first list value starting with that character (if any) and the lookup, when opened, will be positioned at that value.

Deal Number - For a new Quantum deal, do not enter in this field. A unique sequential deal number is automatically assigned by Quantum when you save the deal.

To access an existing Quantum deal, enter the deal number in this field and press Enter (or click the button and select from the list). The details of the deal are displayed.

Ticket Number - Optional deal ticket information - for example, a transaction reference for interfacing to other deal capture systems.

Instrument* - The underlying Letter of Credit Instrument Name; one created in Letter of Credit Instrument Types.

Incoming/Outgoing - Either Incoming - LC if issued by the Counterparty (receipt of monies) OROutgoing - LC if sent to the Counterparty (payment).

Secured - Select or clear this check box to indicate whether the deal is secured or not, respectively.

Counterparty - The Counterparty for the deal. The drop-down list includes all transaction-level Counterparties with Category set to Deal external or Deal internal in Counterparty Setup. The list is filtered on Facility if one is specified.

NOTE: When the Operational Setting Display the counterparty's external name is enabled, a read-only field will also display the External Name entered on the Counterparty record.

Entity - The entity (the legal party) on your side of the deal. The lookup includes all transaction-level Entity records.

NOTE: When the Operational Setting Display the entity's external name is enabled, a read-only field will also display the Entity's External Name.

Facility - Optional. The formal business relationship (Facility or Dealing Line*) that the transaction is recorded against. The default is the setting for the selected Instrument.

  • If Relationship Type is set to No Relationship (in Relationship Control), this field is set to NONE.
  • If Relationship Control requires (or allows) Facilities or Dealing Lines* to be recorded, only those records for which you have selected an allowable combination of Counterparty and Entity are available.
  • *The type selected (FACILITY or DEALING LINE) is indicated in gray above the field.

Currency - select the Currency for the deal from the list of those defined in Currency Setup.

Face Value - The total value that can be presented against the Letter of Credit.

Upper/Lower Tolerance % - The percentages of the Face Value defining the allowed range for the combined presentations.

Maximum/Minimum Amount - The upper and lower limits for any drawdowns against the Letter of Credit; default to Face Value.

Deal Date - The date the deal was transacted; may be a non-business day; defaults to Today's date.

NOTE: Audit Reporting can identify where deal input dates differ from the Deal Date.

Settlement Date - The date on which the Letter of Credit is to take effect; also referred to as the Active From Date.

Maturity Date - The date on which the Letter of Credit ceases to be valid; also referred to as the Expiry Date; cannot be prior to Settlement Date.

Banking Details area - The Issuer, Advisor, Confirmer, and Negotiator Banks, Locations, and Reference numbers, as required.

          For more details of this area, click on an item when the Help panel is displayed.
