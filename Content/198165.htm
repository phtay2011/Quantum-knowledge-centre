Submit Electronic

This function:

  • is NOT available on deal action rows with a Confirmation Method of PAPER or NONE,
  • is available only for applicable Message Status values, and
  • when successfully applied to a Deal Action row, processes and updates that deal action, leaving its CM and AM settings unchanged.

Submit Amended Electronic

This function:

  • is NOT available on deal action rows with a Confirmation Method of PAPER or NONE,
  • is available only for applicable Message Status values, and
  • when successfully applied to a Deal Action row, creates and processes a new deal action row with the same CM and AM settings as the original, leaving the original row settings unchanged.

Filtering

When filtering by this field, you can select PAPER, one or more External Methods, and/or NONE.

Example

You may not want to show the paper confirmation results view row where an electronic one also applies to the same action. From the table above, we see that such results view rows are the only ones where Associated Confirmation Method is NOTPAPER nor NONE. So to exclude those, configure your results view with an Associated Confirmation Method filter specifying one of and selecting PAPER and NONE.

CAUTION: This filtering would also hide a paper row created by applying a Submit Paper function to an electronic results view row (Confirmation Method is an External Method).
