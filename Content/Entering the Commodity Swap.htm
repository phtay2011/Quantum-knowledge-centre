Commodity Swap deal

From Release 6.7.2, Commodity Swap deals are also available (subject to licensing). The following example shows a Commodity Swap deal page.

  1. Open the Commodity Swap dealing page.

  1. On the Deal Details tab, select the Instrument (ALU Fix-Float) - which populates most fields - and enter the Counterparty, Entity, and Fixed Price, as shown below.

  1. To enter a single period, click New.

    The New Period popup window opens.

  2. Enter the dates for August and your required Quantity of 202 MT as shown below, and click OK.

    NOTE:: The Payment Date setting is automatically offset by the specified 2 days, and is highlighted because it is a non-business day. See How-Business Days are determined, below.

    The period is added to the grid.

    • To enter multiple regular periods, you would click Generate Strip instead. The Number of Periods, Frequency, and Start Date Cycling values are defaulted from the instrument settings.
    • A Floating for Floating deal has two sides (Near and Far), and each side is separately priced.

  3. To view pricing for the deal (without fixing the price), first open the Rates tab and set Commodity Rates to LME End of Day, as shown below.

  4. On the Price tab, set the Position Date and Rates Date to 31 July and click Price.

    A Market Value is calculated from the LME End of Day rates and displayed.

  5. Open the Rates tab to view the relevant rates, as shown below.

    TIP: It is assumed that these Commodity Rates had been entered earlier.

  6. Click Save.

    Your deal is saved (as Deal 702 in our example). After a short time, a badge will indicate the Analytics deal identifier (1451 in our example), as shown below.

NOTE: Commodity Swaps is the first deal screen that combines traditional Quantum deal page with Analytics Pricing in one page.
