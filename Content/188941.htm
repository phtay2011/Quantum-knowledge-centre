Interest Rate Option Posting, Profit and Loss tab

  • Premium Expense - This G/L tag is used to record the payment of premium.

    If the Accrue premium check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Selected, the Premium Expense account is debited at deal save, the contra account being Bank Account or Premium Payable (B/S), depending on the settlement date of the premium.
    • Clear, the Premium Expense account is debited when Accrual Inquiry is run in posting mode, the contra account being Premium Expense Accrual (B/S).
  • Premium Income - See Premium Expense in this topic (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Settlement Expense - This G/L tag is used to record the payment of settlement amount resulting from a cash settled option.

    If the Accrue settlement check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Selected, the Settlement Expense account is debited when Accrual Inquiry is run in posting mode, the contra account being Settlement Expense Accrual
    • Clear, the Settlement Expense account is debited at exercise, the contra account being Bank Account or Settlement Payable (B/S), depending on the settlement date.
  • Settlement Income - See Settlement Expense in this topic (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Gain: Realized - This account is credited when a P&L gain is calculated and, debited with a decrease in a P&L gain, the contra account being Revaluation Gain: Realized (B/S).
  • Gain: Unrealized - This G/L tag is used when the applicable Revaluation check box in Accounting Rules is selected.

    This account is credited when a P&L gain is calculated, and debited with a decrease in a P&L gain, the contra account being Revaluation Gain: Unrealized (B/S).

  • Loss: Realized - See Gain: Realized in this topic (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Loss: Unrealized - See Gain: Unrealized in this topic (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • CCY Translation Gain: Realized - There is no currency translation functionality at deal level for Interest Rate Options. This is taken up at the G/L account templates level.
  • CCY Translation Gain: Unrealized - There is no currency translation functionality at deal level for Interest Rate Options. This is taken up at the G/L account templates level.
  • CCY Translation Loss: Realized - There is no currency translation functionality at deal level for Interest Rate Options. This is taken up at the G/L account templates level.
  • CCY Translation Loss: Unrealized - There is no currency translation functionality at deal level for Interest Rate Options. This is taken up at the G/L account templates level.
