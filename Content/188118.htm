Dividends on Unit Holdings

A Dividend payment is a Corporate Action where cash and/or shares are distributed to shareholders in proportion to their equity holding.

Some Dividends are only paid in cash, while others provide a choice to accept the dividend in cash or shares. In Quantum Six, the three possible of Dividend payment types are:

  • DIV PIC MANDATORY - payment in cash only,
  • DIV PIC OPTION - payment in cash if the holder so chooses, or
  • DIV PIK OPTION - payment in kind (in shares), with any remainder amount paid in cash.

A new Dividend payment record can be created (or the latest one opened for editing) from the Unit Holding page.

Use the Unit Dividend page to:

  • enter or edit details of a Dividend Corporate Action on a Unit Holding;
  • view any recent Corporate Action Notifications that have been linked to the Holding and that match the Dividend details;
  • view the cash flows for the Dividend;
  • view the General Ledger entries related to the Dividend;
  • if the Dividend Type is DIV PIK OPTION enter or edit:
    • additional information for the Dividend Unit Deal (and possibly the new holding) that will be created;
    • any fee information applying to the Dividend Unit Deal; and
    • any Analysis Code values required for the Dividend Unit Deal.

To create a new CASH Dividend Corporate Action:

  1. On the Unit Holding page, open the holding you want.
  2. In the page menu, select Log Dividend.

    The Unit Dividend page opens in a new window.

  3. On the Details tab:
    • select a cash (PIC) Dividend Type (DIV PIC MANDATORY or DIV PIC OPTION - see above);
    • enter or edit the Record Date and Dealer;
    • in the Cash Dividend area, select the cash Currency, enter the Dividend Per Unit cash amount, Deal Date, and Settlement Date (the Gross Amount [= Dividend Per Unit x Eligible Balance] and Net Amount [= Gross Amount - Tax deducted] are then displayed);
    • (optional) in the Recent Notifications grid, review any matching Corporate Action Notifications that have previously been linked to the Unit Holding and that match the Dividend Type and Record Date specified.
  4. On the Cash Flows and G/L entries tabs, optionally review the cash flows and G/L entries that will be generated.
  5. Click Save.

    The Unit Dividend page window closes. On the Unit Holding page, your Dividend action has been applied, as shown on the Corporate Actions tab.

To create a new PIK (Payment in Kind) Dividend Corporate Action:

  1. On the Unit Holding page, open the holding you want.
  2. In the page menu, select Log Dividend.
  3. The Unit Dividend page opens in a new window, with the Details tab selected.
  4. Select the Dividend Type of DIV PIK OPTION (see above).
  5. Enter the Ratio or For number as required, and enter or select the Record Date, Deal Date, Settlement Date, and Dealer.
  6. Enter or select the Record Date, Dealer, and, for the Payment-in-Kind Dividend Unit Deal, its Holding Instrument, Security ID, Entity, Deal Date, number of Units, Price, and next Dividend inclusion option (CUM or EX).
  7. In the Settlement area, enter the Gross Settlement amount and Settle On date.
  8. in the Cash Dividend area, select the cash Currency, enter the Dividend Per Unit cash amount, Deal Date, and Settlement Date.

    The Gross Amount and Net Amount values are then displayed.

  9. (Optional) In the Recent Notifications grid, review any matching Corporate Action Notifications that have previously been linked to the Unit Holding and that match the Dividend Type and Record Date specified above.
  10. On the Other Details tab, enter or edit additional reference information relating to the Dividend Unit Deal, one or more Locations to determine business days, and optional text comments.
  11. On the Fees tab, enter or edit any fee information applying to the Dividend Unit Deal.
  12. On the Cash Flows and G/L entries tabs, optionally review the cash flows and G/L entries that will be generated.
  13. On the Analysis Codes tab, select or enter any Analysis Code settings required for the Dividend Unit Deal.
  14. Click Save.

    The Unit Dividend page window closes. Your Dividend action has been applied, as shown on the Corporate Actions tab. The generated Payment-in-Kind Dividend Unit Deal is created and, if a Unit Holding matching its Holding Instrument, Security ID, and Entity:

    • already exists - the new deal is added to that holding, or
    • does not yet exist - , a new Unit Holding is automatically created to match.

To edit the last Dividend for the current holding:

(Only allowed when a Dividend is the most recent Corporate Action completed on the Holding.)

  1. On the Unit Dividend page's Corporate Actions tab, click Edit Last Dividend.

    The Unit Dividend page opens in a new window, displaying the entered details for the last Dividend.

  2. Edit the data items as required (see above).
  3. Click Save.

    The Unit Dividend page window closes. On the Unit Holding page, your changes have been applied, as shown on the Corporate Actions tab.

To delete the last Dividend for the current holding:

(Only allowed when a Dividend is the most recent Corporate Action completed on the Holding.)

  1. On the Unit Holding page's Corporate Actions tab, click Delete Last Dividend.
  2. When prompted, click OK to confirm.

    The Dividend is deleted and no longer shows on the Corporate Actions tab. All database changes caused by the Dividend are backed out.
