Interest Rate Swap Posting, Balance Sheet tab

  • Swap Principal: Pay - Debit Balance Sheet account. This records the swap asset, where physical exchanges of Principal occur. The contra entry is to the bank account.
  • Swap Principal: Receive - Credit Balance Sheet account. This records the swap liability, where physical exchanges of Principal occur. The contra entry is to the bank account.
  • Interest Rate Swap Position - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used for Dual Currency Bonds as the contra entry for the Bond Income on settlement, when it is switched into the coupon currency.
  • Adhoc Amount Accrual - Debit/Credit Balance Sheet account.

    You can customize deals to have cashflows inserted that are additional to Principal and Interest. These may be classified as Adhoc Amounts, or Adhoc Interest. These amounts are intended to be Profit and Loss items rather than capital. Use Adhoc Interest if the amount is to be accrued, and Adhoc Amount if it is to be posted directly to Profit and Loss.

    If the Accrue adhoc amounts check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used, and the accounting treatment is the same for both types. It is posted directly to Profit and Loss.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry. The accruals posting report accrues to the Adhoc Amount default.
  • Bond Discount Accrual: Pay - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
  • Bond Discount Accrual: Receive - Debit Balance Sheet account.

    If the Accrue capital check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it by a bank account entry.
  • Interest Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, the Accruals Posting report creates this entry, and contras it to the Interest Expense (P/L) account.

    On deal save, the accrual entry is posted and contra'd by a bank account entry.

  • Interest Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account. See Interest Expense Accrual.
  • Revaluation Gain: Realized - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where a Gain: Realized (P/L) entry is posted.
  • Revaluation Gain: Unrealized - Debit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where a Gain: Unrealized (P/L) entry is posted.
  • Revaluation Loss: Realized - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where a Loss: Realized (P/L) entry is posted.
  • Revaluation Loss - Unrealized - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is the contra entry where a Loss: Unrealized (P/L) entry is posted.
  • Tax Credit - Credit Balance Sheet account. This is used to represent the Withholding Tax deducted on interest received, that is, a Tax Credit.
  • FID Payable on Borrowing - RESERVED
  • WTAX Expense Accrual - Credit Balance Sheet account.

    This is used to accrue the Withholding Tax on Interest Expense.

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.
  • WTAX on Income Accrual - Debit Balance Sheet account.

    This is used to accrue the Withholding Tax on Interest Income

    If the Accrue interest check box in Accounting Rules is:

    • Cleared, this default is not used.
    • Selected, Quantum creates this entry at deal save time, and contras it to a bank account entry.
