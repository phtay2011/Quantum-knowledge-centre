Initial Margin

(392,464.00)

The Initial Margin amount that needs to be held by the Broker on this date.

Excess/Shortage*

(116,625.27)

The amount still to be added to the account - in this case a shortage to be paid to the Broker (the difference between what is required and what is already there).

* To transfer a shortage to the Broker, use a Cash Transfer.

NOTE: The G/L entries always reflect the adjustment, regardless of whether the Initial Margin deal entry type was an Adjustment or Balance entry. The system maintains an opening balance, and adds or subtracts the actual cash flows. From this, it calculates the excess or shortage.
