Commodities

Quantum Six now enables you to manage the risk exposure for the commodities you trade, between purchase, production, and sale.

With Commodity Swap deals, you can define all aspects of the trade (that is, the commodity's definition, its units of measure, measure conversion for your reporting, and so on). Commodity Swap deals are implemented similarly to other types of Swap deal in Quantum, with the added ability to price them using Analytics market valuations.

With Commodity Exposure deals, you can record and report on your organization's exposure to forecasted purchases and sales of commodities. No trading applies, so no cash flows nor General Ledger entries are generated.

New Static Data types have been introduced to support Commodities - namely, Commodity, Measure, and Measure Conversion), as well as a Commodity rate.

Licensing

Commodities functionality is a separately licensed feature.

History

Previously, commodity trades could only be recorded as FX deals. The Currency static data record was used to register the commodity, which needed to match predefined RiskMetrics codes in Analytics.

TIP: This functionality remains supported for existing clients using it (and for those not licensed for the new Commodities functionality).
