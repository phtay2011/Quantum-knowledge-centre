Linked Deal Analysis window, HR Deal Revaluation

The Linked Deal Analysis window opens when you click an appropriate deal entry in the Retrospective or Prospective tab of the Deal Revaluation page, when a Hedge Relationship (HR) deal is displayed.

This window does not apply when the relevant Test Method is REGRESSION.

The Linked Deal Analysis window displays more detailed revaluation information and calculation details for the selected deal and, where applicable, the Zero Curve data used to calculate market rates.

Information is divided into the following tabs and areas.

Deal Analysis tab

NOTE: If market values need to be adjusted for cash flow movements, then all sections and items are displayed.

Revaluation Information area

  • Deal Number - the system-generated number that identifies the deal
  • Transaction Type:

    AC - Actual Cash Flow
    CO - Currency Option
    FR - FRA
    FU - Futures and exchange Traded Option
    FX - Foreign Exchange (all types)
    IO - Interest Rate Option
    PC - Projected Cash Flow
    SC - Cross Currency Swap
    SE - Money Market OR Security
    SI - Interest Rate Swap

  • Revaluation Method - as specified for the HR deal on the Linked Deals tab of the Hedge Relationship page. Available settings depend on the Transaction Type and the Designated Risk setting for the HR deal - see Revaluation Methods in Hedge Relationships.
  • Position Date - the as at date specified in the header of the Deal Revaluation page; the date to which calculations are made.
  • Rate Date - the date for the rates to be used.
  • Rate Name - the unique identifier for the Rates to be used.
  • Rate Type - the type of rate used.
  • Hedge Rate (displays when an FR* is hedging an MM* or SE* deal) - the dealt rate associated with the FRA deal.

    * See Transaction Type above.

  • Hedge Cap Rate (displays when an IO* is hedging an MM* or SE* deal) - the rate above which a CAP or COLLAR option is hedging.
  • Hedge Floor Rate (displays when an IO* is hedging an MM* or SE* deal) - the rate below which a FLOOR or COLLAR option is hedging.

Fair Value area

This area contains one grid row, giving the Position Date and the following column values.

  • FX Rate Date - the date of the FX rate used to convert the deal from the natural currency to the test currency.
  • Natural (ncy) - the fair value amount in the natural currency ncy.
  • Rate to Test Ccy (tcy) - the FX rate used to convert to the test currency tcy.
  • Test Ccy Amt (tcy) - the fair value amount in the test currency tcy.

    TIP: For linked Swap deals in a FAIR VALUE Hedge Relationship with REGRESSION as the Retrospective Effectiveness method, the Fair Value of the swap is calculated as the sum of BOTH legs.

Settled Cashflows area (MM, SC, SE, and SI deals only - see Transaction Types above)

This area lists any cash flows that have already been settled; one row per cash flow, giving the type of flow (COUPON, INTEREST, and so on), the date, and the following column values.

  • FX Rate Date - the date of the FX rate used to convert the cash flow from natural currency to test currency.
  • Natural (ncy) - the cash flow amount in the natural currency ncy.
  • Rate to Test Ccy (tcy) - the FX rate used to convert to the test currency tcy.
  • Amt (tcy) - the cash flow amount in the test currency tcy.

A final grid row gives the total of the cash flow amounts (in the test currency).

Revaluation area

This area displays the standard Revaluation Analysis details applicable when the deal is revalued outside of a Hedge Relationship. If it is a Swap, the Pay and Receive sides are separately detailed.

Zero Curve tab

This tab lists the Zero Curve data used to calculate market rates for the linked deal, as applicable and when available for the specified rate and date.
