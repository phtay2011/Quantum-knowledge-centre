Currency Option Posting, Balance Sheet tab

  • Premium Payable - This G/L tag is used if the premium is payable on a date other than the Deal date.

    The account is credited at deal save, the Contra account being one of the following, depending on the settings in Accounting Rules:

    • Premium Expense Accrual - Defer premium or Accrue premium
    • Premium Expense - Expense premium

    The account is debited on the Premium Settlement date, the Contra account being Bank Account.

  • Premium Receivable - See Premium Payable above (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Rebate Payable - This G/L tag is used if the rebate is payable on a date other than the Deal date.

    The account is credited when an option is knocked out, the Contra account being one of the following, depending on the settings in Accounting Rules:

    • Rebate Expense Accrual - Defer premium or Accrue premium, or...
    • Rebate Expense - Expense premium.

    The account is debited on the Rebate Settlement date, the contra account being Bank Account.

  • Rebate Receivable - See Rebate Payable above (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Settlement Payable - This G/L tag is used when an option is cash settled, and Settlement date is on a date other than the Deal date.

    The account is credited at Exercise date, the contra account being Loss: Realized (P/L) Account.

    The account is debited on the cash Settlement date, the contra account being Bank Account.

  • Settlement Receivable - See Settlement Payable above (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Premium Expense Accrual - This G/L tag is used with either of the following Revaluation settings in Accounting Rules:
    • Defer premium
    • Accrue premium

    The account is debited at deal save, the Contra account being either Bank Account or Premium Payable, depending on the Settlement date of the premium.

    The account is credited when the Accruals query is run in posting mode, the Contra account being Premium Expense (P/L).

  • Premium Income Accrual - See Premium Expense Accrual above (reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Rebate Expense Accrual - This G/L tag is used when the Apply Rebate Accrual option is selected in Accounting Rules.

    The account is debited when an option is knocked out, the Contra account being either Bank Account or Rebate Payable, depending on the Settlement date of the rebate.

    The account is credited when the Accruals query is run in posting mode, the Contra account being Rebate Expense (P/L).

  • Rebate Income Accrual - See Rebate Expense Accrual. (Reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Base Reval Gain: Realized - This G/L tag is used with the following Revaluation settings in Accounting Rules:
    • Posting method - Realized
    • Revaluation currency - Base

    This account is debited when a P&L gain is calculated, and credited with a decrease in a P&L Gain, the contra account being Base Gain: Realized (P/L).

  • Base Reval Gain: Unrealized - This G/L tag is used with the following Revaluation settings in Accounting Rules:
    • Posting method - Unrealized
    • Revaluation currency - Base

    This account is debited when a P&L gain is calculated, and credited with a decrease in a P&L gain, the contra account being Base Gain: Unrealized (P/L).

  • Base Reval Loss: Realized - See Base Reval Gain: Realized (Reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Base Reval Loss: Unrealized - See Base Reval Gain: Unrealized. (Reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Revaluation Gain: Realized - This G/L tag is used with the following Revaluation settings in Accounting Rules:
    • Posting method - Realized
    • Revaluation currency - Non base or Premium

    This account is debited when a P&L gain is calculated, and credited with a decrease in a P&L Gain, the contra account being Gain: Realized (P/L).

  • Revaluation Gain: Unrealized - This G/L tag is used with the following Revaluation settings Accounting Rules:
    • Posting method - Unrealized
    • Revaluation currency - Non base or Premium

    This account is debited when a P&L gain is calculated, and credited with a decrease in a P&L gain, the contra account being Gain: Unrealized (P/L).

  • Revaluation Loss: Realized - See Revaluation Gain: Realized. (Reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
  • Revaluation Loss: Unrealized - See Revaluation Gain: unrealized (Reverse the direction of the accounting entries).
