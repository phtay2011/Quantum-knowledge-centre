Linked Deals tab, Hedge Relationship

Use this tab to add, auto-allocate, edit, and remove underlying deals making up a Hedge Relationship.

Your settings for Hedge/Exposure, Revaluation Method, and Allocation % for each linked deal are crucial to achieve the desired accounting result and assess effectiveness. For more details see Hedge Accounting Setup Requirements and Revaluation Methods in Hedge Relationships.

  • Interest Rate Hedges - These can only hedge floating Money Market or Security deals; and you cannot mix different types of Interest Rate Hedges in the same Hedge Relationship - for example, a Hedge Relationship cannot contain a FRA Hedge and an Interest Rate Option Hedge.
  • Commodity Hedges - Editing the period of a deal that is designated in the Deal Details tab as a Commodity Price Risk will edit all linked deals. And, be careful allocating risk for a period: we will warn you if your edit makes total risk allocation > 100%, but won't warn if the total allocation is below 100%.

To add (designate) a new linked deal - Select any of the following tab menu functions:

  • Add by Deal / Projected Cash Flow... - enter the numbers, separated by commas or spaces, and click OK.
  • Add by Query - enter the Entity, From and To Date, select Include Unapproved Deals if applicable; optionally enter characters in the Search box to narrow the search; select deals in the results list; and click OK.

    The selected deals and their default settings are listed in the Linked Deals grid, including periods for Commodity Swaps and Commodity Exposures. Then, below the grid:

    • The Face Value Ratio and Ratio Dates values gives the ratio of Hedge to Exposure for all linked deals.
    • If the Linked deals include an Interest Rate Option (IO) or Forward Rate Agreement (FR), the applicable Hedge Rate is also displayed (Floor and Cap rates if a Collar IO).

To edit a linked deal - click a data value in the deal row or select the row and Edit in the tab menu; in the Edit dialog box edit the settings as required; and click OK.

To remove a linked deal (provided it has not been included in a Position Processing run) - select the deal row; in the tab menu select Delete; and in the dialog box click OK to confirm (or in the Edit dialog box, click Delete and OK to confirm).

To remove (de-designate) individual deals, periods within a deal, or the HR - Select Dedesignate Deals or Dedesignate Hedge Relationship in the page menu. You can dedesignate a linked deal in full or partial.
