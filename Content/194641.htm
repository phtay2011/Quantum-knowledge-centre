Futures Variation Margin - Payment delay

(First introduced in Release SP 6.7.1, August 2018.)

You can now delay the payment date for settlement of the Variation Margin in Quantum Futures deals.

Implementation

Futures and ETOs page, Instruments menu

A Margin Settlement Offset field now appears on the Futures Contract Details tab, as highlighted below. Optionally enter a small number of business days after Settlement, to specify when the Variation Margin will be paid for Futures deals based on that instrument. The default is blank (zero, meaning no delay).

Futures and ETOs page, Dealing menu

For a deal based on a Futures and ETOs instrument specifying a non-zero Margin Settlement Offset (as above), the payment delay will be indicated on the relevant Deal Map, Cash Flows, and G/L Entries tab items and G/L entries will show the recording and clearing down across a Margin Receivable or Margin Payable account, as applicable.

Posting Rule page

Futures & ETOs tab Posting Rule entries now include Margin Payable and Margin Receivable Balance Sheet account fields, as highlighted below. These enable posting of Variation Margin expense and income to specific Balance Sheet accounts if required, when a Variation Margin Payment Delay applies (if there is no margin delay, these accounts are not involved, and the posting is direct from Cash to P&L or vice versa, as applicable).

History

Previously, Variation Margin payment was always on the Settlement Date, and there was therefore no requirement to specify Margin Payable/Receivable Balance Sheet accounts in Posting Rule entries for Futures and ETOs.
