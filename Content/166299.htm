Reapply Cash Flows and Accounting

CAUTION: Before using this utility, consult your FIS Consultant.

When you change Accounting Setup and/or Bank Account default settings in Quantum Six, all deals saved from that time on will use the new settings. Postings and cash flows dated before the change remain the same, reflecting the defaults that applied when those transactions were first saved.

In certain situations, it may be necessary to update existing transactions (including deal cash flows) when you change Accounting Setup and/or Bank Account defaults. The Reapply Cash Flows and Accounting utility page enables you to achieve this.

When you run the utility, the system reverses or deletes existing G/L entries and reapplies them using your newly defined defaults. You can also optionally reapply cash flows to reflect changes to Bank Account defaults, without affecting customized cash flows or closeouts on deals.

  • Quantum does not re-save the cash flows or recalculate the deal, it only re-stamps the bank account information on cash flows based on the current bank account defaults. If necessary, G/L entries are also changed to reflect bank account changes.
  • If G/Ls are reapplied then you must re-run accruals, revaluations, and currency translation, as all existing G/L entries will have been removed or reversed.

The utility settings and options enable you to select the transactions or deals to reapply, and to control the reversal or deletion of old transactions. You can:

  • specify transaction selection criteria in terms of the original deal Entity, Transaction Type, and/or Instrument. Alternatively, you can select specific deals by Deal Number,
  • alternatively -to facilitate Hedge Accounting - select deals that mature after a specified date (the date when Hedge Accounting is introduced),
  • (if you are reapplying cash flows) choose whether to reapply your own Bank Account defaults (NOSTRO), or those for Counterparties (VOSTRO), or both,
  • suppress any warning messages that may arise during processing,
  • choose whether to post to the Suspense account on errors, and/or
  • choose whether to reverse or delete the existing G/L transactions before creating new entries for a G/L End of Year,

    CAUTION: Using the Delete Existing Entries option on live databases is not recommended as it may cause serious problems with your data.

    If a G/L End of Year has been run, deals entered before that process will be reversed even if you choose the Delete Existing Entries option.

Exclusions

The reapplying process does not:

  • reapply changed Instrument settings (for example, Days Basis). Only G/Ls, Accounting Rule settings, and Bank Accounts are re-saved based on the current default records,
  • recalculate cash flows when changes to business holidays occur (customized deals are retained),
  • enable you to add or change deal details such as Analysis Codes, Strategy, Issuers, or Relationships,
  • check for any closed accounting periods and passed effective dates,
  • check whether cash flows are settled, nor
  • include detailed checks similar to those made by Bank/Counterparty Bank Account Conversion.
