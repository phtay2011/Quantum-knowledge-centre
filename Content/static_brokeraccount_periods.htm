Periods area

(Available only when Calculate Interest is YES on this tab and, for Group-level accounts, Interest Group is selected.)

The Periods area contains the following fields.

Frequency - the period for the interest calculations on this account; select one of:

  • DAILY - to calculate interest on an overnight basis;
  • MONTHLY - to calculate interest on a monthly basis, up to the Interest Period Ends day;
  • QUARTERLY, SEMI-ANNUALLY, or ANNUALLY - to calculate interest on the specified basis, starting from the Effective Date month, up to the Interest Period Ends day; OR
  • ACCOUNTING PERIODS (not available for Net Group level accounts) - where Interest calculation periods are as defined in the applicable Accounting Period record.

Interest Period Ends - the relative end date of interest periods.

  • Select 10TH OF MONTH and enter the actual date if the interest period ends on a specific day in the final month of each period.
  • Select END OF MONTH if the period ends on the last day of the final month in each period.

Cash Flow Due - Specify the date on which the interest cash flow is to occur - either a valid date expression OR one of the following values, depending on the Interest Period Frequency setting.

When theFrequency is DAILY:

  • Select SAME DAY to process the interest cash flow on the day for which it is calculated.
  • Select NEXT DAY to process the interest cash flow on the following day.

For all other Interest Periods:

  • Select 10TH OF MONTH and enter the actual date if Quantum should pay the Interest on a specified date of the month in which the interest period ends.

    NOTE: You cannot set this date to be earlier in the month than the Interest Period End date.

  • Select 10TH OF FOLLOWING MONTH and enter the actual date if Quantum should pay the Interest on a specified date of the month following the end of the interest period.
  • Select END OF MONTH if Quantum should pay the Interest on the last day of the month in which the interest period ends.
  • Select END OF FOLLOWING MONTH if Quantum should pay the Interest on the last day of the month following the month following the end of the interest period.

    NOTE: For the End of Month rules, if the last day of the month falls on a non-business day, then the interest is posted on the first business day of the following month.

Allow Cash Flows on Non Business Days - Select this check box if you want the bank interest cash flow to occur on the Cash Flow Due date, even when this is a non business day. Clear this check box if you want bank interest cash flows to occur according to your defined Business Days Convention, when the Cash Flow Due date is a non business day. For example, your business convention may be to process transactions on the following business day.

Include Zero Balance - This setting controls whether or not the Bank Account is to be marked as included in an interest period* if the balance or interest rate is zero (for the applicable Effective Date Interest record).

*NOTE: The setting on the 3rd Party Interest tab applies to third-party interest periods (if applicable). It may differ from the setting on the Interest tab.

Select the Include Zero Balance check box if, when the account has a zero balance or zero percent interest, you still want:

  • The Bank Account Interest Date to be updated when interest is posted,

    -and-

  • The Bank Account Interest Accrual Date to be updated if and when interest is accrued.

    NOTES:

    • No Bank Account Interest deal is created if the balance or interest rate is zero - whether this option is selected or not. The option simply determines whether or not the account is marked as having been included in a particular interest period for interest accruals or interest postings.
    • Once the Effective Date record has been used for posting of interest (even if it was based on a zero balance), you can no longer change the details of this Effective Date record, and Include Zero Balance becomes read-only.
    • By default, this option is cleared.
