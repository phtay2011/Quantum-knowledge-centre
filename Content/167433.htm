FRA

Use the FRA Deal page to create and edit Forward Rate Agreement (FRA) transactions.

TIPS: A Forward Rate Agreement is an agreement between two parties to hedge against or to speculate on a movement in future interest rates.

External References

External Reference numbers and text will appear as read-only values on the Deal Details tab, but only when:

  • the deal data has been imported or input via an API, and
  • the External Reference fields or properties are populated.

Deals in Hedge Relationships

TIP:

  • To add and maintain any deals in a Hedge Relationship, use the Linked Deals tab on the Hedge Relationship page.
  • The Dealing page shows a Hedge Relationships tab if the current deal is a Linked Deal within one or more Hedge Relationships. If so, you can quickly access the Hedge Relationship from this tab by clicking on the HR Deal Number in the Allocations grid.

Calculating FRA Notional (Hedge) Periods

The Notional Period (also known as the Hedge Period) is the time over which the difference between Dealt and Fixed Interest rates is applied to calculate the Settlement Amount.

Quantum determines the the Notional Period as follows.

  • if the FRA is In Arrears - from Fix Date to Maturity Date, or
  • if the FRA is In Advance - from Settlement Date to Maturity Date.

    TIP: For Advance FRAs, the Settlement Date is often the same as the Fix Date, but not always.

Fixing the Rate for FRA Deals

FRAs are 'fixed' against a predetermined market basis on an agreed date, as recorded on the deal record (provided the deal has been approved) The FRA Rate Fix action may be initiated from the:

  • FRA Deal page,
  • Settlements Processing page (on one or more FRA deal cash flows), or
  • Rate Set Deals Processing page (on multiple FRA deals).

The FRA Fix action calculates the Settlement amount (which may be overwritten) and then generates a cash flow on the FRA deal.
