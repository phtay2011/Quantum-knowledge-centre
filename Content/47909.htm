Weighted Average Closeout formulas

NOTE: You can use the Weighted Average Closeout Processing page as an alternative to selecting the WEIGHTED AVG Closeout Method on the Securities Instrument page.

These formulas apply only to Securities instruments where:

  • Accounting Control details for the instrument specify Adjusted Book Value.
  • Accounting Control details for the Instrument do not specify Capital Accruals.
  • The Closeout Method for the instrument is set to WEIGHTED AVG.

'Normal' v 'Closeout' Deals

In this context, a normal deal is one that adds to the value of the portfolio - either a new or initial purchase on a long position, or a new sell on a short position.

A Closeout deal is one that subtracts from the value of the portfolio - either a sell on a long position or a buy on a short position.
