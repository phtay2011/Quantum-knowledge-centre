Deal Details tab, Unit Deal

Use this tab to enter, edit, or review the main details for the Unit Deal, as follows.

Counterparty (applicable and mandatory when the underlying Holding Instrument specifies a Trading Style of Over the Counter (OTC)) - The Counterparty that this deal (and any others in the holding) are bought from or sold to.

Broker (applicable and mandatory when the underlying Holding Instrument specifies a Trading Style of Exchange Traded) - The clearing broker handling the deal (and any others in the holding). The lookup lists Counterparties with Applicable Categories of bothClearing Broker and Deal External selected.

Entity (mandatory) - The entity owning the holding (the shareholder organization); defaulted from the underlying Holding Instrument.

Corporate Action Counterparty (mandatory; read-only if a holding already exists for the specified Holding Instrument, Security ID, and Entity combination) - The Counterparty for associated Corporate Actions. With an OTC Trading Style, this may or may not be the same as Counterparty. For example, if Dividends are paid by a Counterparty other than the one the Units were bought from, this would be the Dividend-paying Counterparty.

Buy/Sell - Indicates whether the deal is a purchase (Buy) or sale (Sell).

Units - The number of Units transacted in this deal. When calculated by the system, the number of units will be rounded down for BUY deals and up for SELL deals.

Price (mandatory, but not initially entered where an Estimated Price applies) - The actual Unit price for this deal. If an Estimated Priceis used initially, an adjustment is made when this price is entered.

Estimated Price (applicable and mandatory only when, in the underlying Holding Instrument, Trading Style is Over the CounterandAllow Estimated Prices Y/N is selected) - The Estimated Price for the Unit Deal. When the actual price is entered, an adjustment is then made (either directly to the Gross Settlement Amount or as a separate Adjustment Amount cash flow).

Dividend - Indicates whether or not the deal will qualify for the next dividend payment; one of:

  • Cum - when the deal date is before the next Ex Dividend date (whether declared or not): the deal will qualify for the next dividend, or
  • Ex - when the deal date is between a declared Ex Dividend date and the Dividend payout date: the deal units will not qualify for that dividend.

Deal Date, Hours, Minutes, Seconds - The date and time the deal was transacted; defaults to Today's date and the current system time, but you may enter a different time and/or a prior or future date(which may be a non-business day).
