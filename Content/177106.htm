G/L Accounting Process Summary

General Ledger Close

You can record when each accounting period is closed. This ensures that the system maintains the accounting period concept, so that edited or backdated deals that affect a closed period are properly managed in regard to the period in which they are reported.

Any transaction or entry recorded after an accounting period is closed, and dated on or before the close date, is posted on the first calendar day after the close date.

This is not a mandatory process, but provides a measure of control over accounting data. There are two levels of close available: Interim and Full.

CAUTION: Quantum applications review accounting periods (and so detect newly closed periods) when they are started. Any posting programs running at the time a period is closed will therefore remain able to post back-dated entries into the closed period until such time as they are restarted.

For this reason, it is recommended that all users exit from posting applications (such as those accessed via Dealing and Processing pages) before running G/L Close.

Accruals

Accruals processing accrues and amortizes the value of interest, premium, discount and forward points income and expenses, posting to the G/L on an incremental basis - usually as at the end of the accounting period.

Revaluations

Position processing calculates and posts Mark-to-Market revaluations on deals in accordance with the Accounting Rules and optionally processes Hedge Relationships - usually as at the end of the accounting period and following Accruals processing.

If necessary, mark-to-market revaluations can be discounted to record the change in the Net Present Value of the gain or loss.

Currency Translation

Currency Translation (Currency Translation Processing, Currency Translation) calculates and posts the Foreign Exchange gains and losses on balance sheet items - usually as at the end of the accounting period and following Accruals and Position processing.

These gains and losses arise when the system records foreign currency amounts in the G/L at a different rate from the rate at settlement.

At deal level, the currency translation occurring up until the settlement is Unrealized, and this becomes Realized at settlement. At balance sheet account level, the system determines and posts the Unrealized exchange variation on the balance of the account.

Year End Posting

G/L Year End Posting, as the last action at the end of each financial year:

  • posts the balances of all Profit and Loss accounts to a Balance Sheet account (Retained Earnings) to reflect the net gain or loss for the year, and
  • creates G/L Journal entries for all Profit & Loss accounts - for the actual currency and for the base currency equivalent amount.

Hedge Accounting

Hedge Relationships tests effectiveness and revalues deals in accordance with the applicable rules.

Weighted Average Closeout Processing

Weighted Average Closeout Processing Enables portfolio-based weighted average closeout (WAC) processing.

NOTE: The WAC Processor program provides an alternative weighted average closeout process to the standard Quantum weighted average closeout method set up in Money Market Accounting Rules.

Futures Margin Processing

Futures Margin Processing revalues Futures and ETO deals and posts to the G/L.

TIP: Position processing also revalues Futures deals, but does not post to the G/L.

General Ledger Journal Entries

Use the General Ledger Journal page to manually record journal entries that are not transaction-related, and hence not generated automatically by deal entry or other processes.

Set Base Currency Amounts

Setting Base Currency Amounts enables you to improve efficiency of General Ledger (G/L) reporting, by:

  • calculating equivalent amounts in the appropriate G/L Owner's Base Currency for every foreign currency G/L entry, and
  • updating G/L Account Balances in their Base Currency for backdated FX Rate changes.

Assign Exchange Rates

Assigning Exchange Rates enables you to specify exchange rates for creating base currency G/L entries for foreign currency Money Market deals.

Override FX Rate Types

Overriding FX Rate Types enables you to override the transaction rates for FX deals, translating the natural currency G/L entries to their base currency equivalent.
