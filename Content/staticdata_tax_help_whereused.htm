Where Taxes are Specified

Use the Tax Maintenance page to enter and maintain transactional tax records.

These taxes can then be specified or applied in the following areas of Quantum.

Entity page

You can link a transactional tax to an Entity in your Business Structure.

Select the default tax rate record that will be used for WHT or VAT calculation on the Entity's Money Market, Securities, and Interest Rate Swap deals.

Money Market and Securities Instrument page

On the Taxes tab, you can specify taxes from which particular Money Market or Security deals are exempt, and select options (where applicable) for:

  • Separate tax cash flows to be generated on a partial or full Unwind (YIELD instruments with Timing of On Maturity, Periodic, or Rolling)
  • WHT on Accrued Interest (Purchased Income) and/or Capital Discount/Premium, which apply only to instruments with the following settings:
    • Formula = BOND, Timing = PERIODIC, Callable option cleared

      -or-
    • Formula = FRN-CGL, Asset Backed option cleared, Multiple Rateset option cleared)

Swap Instrument page

On the Taxes tab, you can specify one or more transactional taxes from which deals based on that instrument are exempt.

For a Swap leg based on a Money Market Instrument, the Money Market Instrument options above also apply to that leg.

Counterparty page

On the Taxes tab, you can specify one or more transactional taxes for which the Counterparty is liable, and the tax number for each investor.

Cash Account and Broker Account pages

On the Interest tab, you can specify a Withholding Tax for the bank or broker account, and whether tax is on Net or Gross interest payment total.

Money Market and Securities Dealing pages

On the Other details tab for a new deal, the Taxes field's default settings are derived from the taxes (if any) specified for the deal Entity and Counterparty, with exempt taxes removed if any are specified on the Instrument.

However, you can override these defaults for specific deals. The following validation rules apply.

  • Only one withholding tax may be specified (a tax record with Tax Type set to AIL, NON RESIDENT WHT, or RESIDENT WHT).
  • When applicable*, a Value-Added Tax (a tax record with Tax Type set to VAT) must be the only tax.

*TIP: VAT applies only where the selected Instrument has a Formula Timing setting of:

  • YIELD PERIODIC,
  • PRINCIPAL & INTEREST ACTUAL END DATE, or
  • PRINCIPAL & INTEREST NOTIONAL END DATE.

Bank Interest Statements Reporting and Processing

The system automatically calculates cash flows for payments of Withholding Tax on interest paid on call accounts (debt instruments).
