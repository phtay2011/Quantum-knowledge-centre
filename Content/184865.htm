Accounts grid (Bank Conversion)

The Accounts grid displays all the bank accounts that meet your Account Filters criteria.

Status - Displays the status of the account item in the conversion run.

  • Pending indicates that the item will be processed when the program is restarted.
  • Complete indicates that the item has been processed and all cash flows for the item have been successfully moved.
  • Unknown indicates that the conversion of the item was started but no response was received. This could mean the conversion is still running or that it did not finish due to an unknown error. The conversion will roll back to the original unconverted information. You can then re-run it.
  • Partial indicates that the conversion of the item was successful and returned a status that indicated some (but not all) of the cash flows for the item were processed. The Message Log page will show any errors that were found.

From Bank - Displays the Bank Name of the From Account.

Account Number - Displays the Account Number of the From Account.

Currency - Displays the Currency of the From Account.

Flows - Displays the number of cash flows that will be converted. This count is based on the From Account and the criteria set by the Effective Date and Cash Flow Filters (if applicable). The Flows count will refresh if the Effective Date or any Cash Flow Filters (if applicable) are modified.

To Bank - The Bank Name of the To Account you are moving cash flows to. These may be internal or external bank counterparties.

Account Number - The Account Number of the To Account. Selections you made in the To Bank and Currency will filter the Account Number selections.

Currency - Displays the currency of the To Account for the Bank specified in the To Bank column.

Default Payment Method - Displays all the payment methods applicable for the To Account detailed by the combination of the previous three columns.

Entity Identifier - Optional. Displays the Entity Identifier of all entities with the specified Payment Method/s.

Transfer Balance - Indicates if the balance is to be transferred from the From Account to the To Account. An account transfer deal is created if there is a balance to transfer.

Bank Acct Transfer Instrument - The account transfer instrument to use if an account transfer deal is to be created. Visible only if Transfer Balance is selected.
