Accounting Treatments/Exclusions for Equities/Mutual Funds

The following accounting treatment has been implemented for Units (based on FAS 115, IAS32, and IAS39).

NOTES:

  • This is done in Quantum by using different Accounting Rules and Entities, and is not a classification directly on the deal.
  • The Equity Method is excluded (applies when an investor owns more than 20% of the stock in a corporation)

Accounting Treatments

The accounting treatments can be split into the following classifications.

  • Available for sale
  • Trading

Accounting Treatment for 'Available for Sale' and 'Trading'

  • Recorded at purchase price
  • Revaluation gain/loss shown as unrealized
  • Sale of units is shown as a realized gain/loss in earnings
  • Dividend is recognized in earnings when earned

  • 'Available for sale'. Unrealized revaluation gain/(loss) is recognized in shareholders equity by assigning a shareholders equity template (B/S) to Unrealized Gain/(Loss) Profit and Loss entries under Posting Rules.
  • 'Trading'. Unrealized revaluation gain/(loss) is recognized in earnings by assigning an earnings Profit and Loss template to Unrealized Gain/(Loss) Profit and Loss entries under Posting Rules.

Exclusions

  • Currency Translation (Currency Translation Processing, Currency Translation) will not be calculated at the Deal level. It is calculated as Unrealized Gain/(Loss) at the Account level only.
  • Classification of Securities is not supported. This exclusion includes the G/L entries required when changing the classification.
