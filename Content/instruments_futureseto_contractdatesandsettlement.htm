Contract Dates and Settlement area, Futures and ETO Instrument

Use these fields to specify the following settings for entry of Futures and ETO Deals when the selected Contract and Exchange combination applies (for either the Futures or Option contract, depending on whether this is the Futures or Options Contract Dates tab).

Day Rule fields - select the Final Trading Day Rules* that will be used to calculate the days for:

  • Closing (Futures contracts only) OR Expiry (Options contracts only)
  • Settlement
  • First Notice and Last Notice (Futures contract only)

    *TIP: Day rules are defined using the Futures Final Trading Days page.

Settlement Method - select either CASH SETTLED or DELIVERABLE. For more details, see Settlement Method settings, Futures and ETO Instrument.

Mid Curve Years (Options contracts only) - the offset between the Option contract date and the closing date of the underlying Futures contract, expressed in YEARS. For a standard (non Mid Curve) Option, leave this field blank or enter zero. See Mid Curve Options, Futures and ETO Instrument.

Show Trading session field - select this option to display a Trading Session field on the Futures and ETO Deals page, so that dealers can record one (for example Morning, Afternoon, or Overnight).
