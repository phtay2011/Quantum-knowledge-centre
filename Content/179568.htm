Importing Bank Account Transfers

The Bank Account Transfers Deal Importer supports the following QID-format fields.

Mandatory fields

  • bankacc1
  • entity1
  • bankacc2
  • entity2
  • cflowstype1
  • face_value
  • cflowstype2
  • sectype
  • dealer
  • deal_dt

Optional fields

  • bank1
  • bank2
  • ccy
  • comments
  • external_ref_id
  • external_ref_text
  • settle_dt
  • ticket_no

NOTE:

  • Since the defaulting behavior of Settlement Date depends on the Instrument selected, sectype= should precede settle_dt= in the import file
  • Source details are identified by the number 1, Destination details by the number 2.
  • The setting in the cflowstype field determines whether Bank Account or Broker Account information is displayed. You can choose a different setting for each side of the transfer.

Example

(QID format)

EXEName=BAXFER
$DoMenu=mfnew
ticket_no=test001
face_value=10000.00
sectype=AT Instrument1
deal_dt=20-Apr-2007
settle_dt=20-Apr-2007
dealer=IM Porter
deal_set_id=0
external_ref_id=0
comments=
cflowstype1=ACTUAL
bankacc1=66663333
entity1=Transactor - Level 2
cflowstype2=ACTUAL
bankacc2=1000 1000 1000 1000
entity2=Transactor - Level 2
$DoMenu=mfInsert
