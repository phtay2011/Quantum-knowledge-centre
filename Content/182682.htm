Range Accrual (Money Market) Formulas

Analytics

This section describes formulas used for pricing Range Accruals.

The payoff structure of a Range Accrual Note can be seen as a series of long digital call options on the lower rate in the range, and short calls on the upper, with the settlement of these options deferred to the coupon payment date. Therefore there are two significant components to the valuation:

  • valuing a string of digital caplets, and
  • performing a convexity adjustment on the forward rates used in the above valuation, to account for the deferred settlement.

These steps provide the probability that the forward rate for any given date will fall within the specified range. The Enhanced Rate for the period is then adjusted based on this probability, before the expected interest cash flow is projected.
