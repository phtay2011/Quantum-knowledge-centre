Bank Account Monitoring

Use this page to specify bank accounts that may be monitored.

Monitoring is activated when the Monitor bank accounts option on the Operational Settings page is selected. For each bank account being monitored, you can use a specific API call to access the captured data.

TIP: If the Monitor bank accounts option is cleared, a Not Operational badge is displayed on the Bank Account Monitoring page.

For each bank account listed, you can configure monitoring for: End of Day Balances and/or Import Statements (or neither).

To add bank accounts and configure monitoring

  1. Click Add.
  2. Select the bank account/s to be monitored.
  3. Click OK.

    The accounts are listed in the grid. By default, no monitoring options are selected, and they are listed in the Unassigned Options column.

  4. To configure monitoring of End of Day balances and/or Import Statements for an account:
    1. Select the account row in the grid and in the grid menu, select Edit (or click a linked item in the row).
    2. In the Edit Bank Accounts dialog box, select or clear the Monitor EOD Balance and/or Monitor Import Status options as required, and click OK.

    Your selected and cleared monitoring options are displayed in the Assigned Options and Unassigned Options columns, respectively.

  5. Repeat Step 4 for other accounts, as required.
  6. Save your changes.

TIP: To deactivate monitoring of a bank account, clear both options in the Edit Bank Accounts dialog box (to deactivate monitoring for all bank accounts, clear the Monitor bank accounts option on the Operational Settings page).
