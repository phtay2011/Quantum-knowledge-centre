Accrue Option Premium (Interest Rate Option Accounting Rule)

When this option is selected:

The Premium is taken to the Balance Sheet at deal save and expensed to Profit and Loss when Accrual Processing is run. The STRAIGHT LINE method i used to calculate the accrual amount at a daily rate over the specified Premium Accrual Period (NOTIONAL, EXERCISE, or FULL).

You must also select the Premium Translation date (POSTING DATE or CASHFLOW DATE).

NOTE: The following examples show premium payable. If it is receivable, opposite entries and Income accounts apply instead of Expense accounts.

When this option is cleared:

No accruals are required for the Premium. On deal G/L creation the Premium is taken straight to the Profit and Loss account. The date this occurs depends on whether it is paid in advance or in arrears as defined for the deal's underlying Instrument.
