Futures Final Trading Day Examples

Quantum Futures Final Trading Day rule settings for some prominent Futures Exchanges and contracts are given below, plus an example of an Exception rule.

Chicago Board of Trade Wheat Futures

The final trading day is the seventh business day preceding the last business day of the delivery month.

Analytics

Chicago Board of Trade 2-Year US Treasury Note Futures

The final trading day is the eighth-to-last business day of the delivery month.

In this example, the Month End is determined first and then offset by 8 preceding days. EXPIRY (Quantum)or Business Day (Analytics) refer to the date found from the Primary rule fields in each case.

EUREX Option on 3-Month Interest Rate Future

The final trading day is two business days prior to the third Wednesday of the delivery month.

Analytics

LIFFE 3-Month Sterling (Short Sterling) Interest Rate Future

The final trading day is the third Wednesday of the delivery month.

Example of an Exception Rule

For all months except December, the final trading day the last day of the month if that is a business day, otherwise the next business day.

For December, the final trading day is the 24th if that is a business day, otherwise the preceding business day.
