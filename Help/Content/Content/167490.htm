Deal Save and Reversal logic in Quantum

Reversals in Quantum cover actions on a deal. This means that any change made to a deal contract (for example, a change to Settlement Date, Maturity Date, Face Value, or rates) will cause full G/L reversal.

There are two levels of reversal logic in Quantum: Smart Deal Save and Smart G/L Reversal and Deletion.

Smart Deal Save in Quantum

The Quantum Smart Deal Save logic is applied to all actions on a deal. This logic is built-in and therefore cannot be deactivated by a user.

When you re-save a deal, the system recreates only the flows you have actually changed. This minimizes the likelihood of affecting settled cash flows (and stock flows, where relevant), and reduces the time taken to re-save the deal.

If the system identifies changes that affect Confirmations, settled flows, and/or reconciled Bank accounts, a message is displayed notifying you of these before the deal is re-saved.

You can then choose whether to cancel your changes or continue saving them. If you continue, you must then review the changed flows, and make adjustments to correct the resulting discrepancies.

The system also determines whether or not a particular customization warrants a change in the accounting basis of a deal. In an increasing number of circumstances, the original settings are retained.

Smart Deal Save and G/L Entries

This Smart Deal Save process also includes G/L entries. Where the change:

  • does not impact existing G/L entries - no reversals will occur, or
  • does impact existing G/L entries - all of the entries are reversed. The action may impact only one cash flow that has been partially accrued, but the G/L entries for all of the cash flows, accruals, revaluations and currency translation on the deal will be reversed.

The main scenarios that trigger full G/L reversals include back-dated actions and deals with multiple rate sets per interest period.

Smart G/L Reversal and Deletion

The system-wide option Disable smart G/L reversal and deletion option is available (to authorized users) on the Operational Settings page (under Accounting). To implement Smart G/L Reversal and Deletion, clear this option and save the change.

Smart G/L Reversal and Deletion applies only to accrual G/L entries directly affected by a change. It is applied on top of the Smart Deal Save logic.

  • When a change occurs that impacts some or all of the accruals posted, the smart reversal process will reverse (or delete) the accrual GL entries back to that change point - that is, it will not reverse the accrual G/L entries to the start of the deal.
  • The reversal will bring all accrual components back to the same point. This means that all accrual items will reverse, not just the item impacted by the action. For example, if there are capital and interest accrual components and the change impacts only the interest accrual, both the Capital and Interest components will still be reversed to the same point.
  • Smart G/L Reversal and Deletion is currently available only on FRA, Swap, Money Market, and Security deals using the Straight Line or Amortized Cost accrual method, as defined in the applicable Accounting Rules.
